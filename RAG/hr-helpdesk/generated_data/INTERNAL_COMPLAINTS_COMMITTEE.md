# Company Data Protection Policy

**1. Purpose**

This Company Data Protection Policy outlines Acme Corp's commitment to the lawful, fair, and transparent processing of personal data belonging to employees, customers, partners, and other stakeholders. This policy ensures that Acme Corp adheres to the highest standards of data privacy and security in accordance with applicable Indian data protection laws, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDPA").

**2. Scope**

This policy applies to all Acme Corp employees, contractors, consultants, temporary staff, and any third parties acting on behalf of Acme Corp who collect, process, store, or transmit personal data. It covers all personal data processed by Acme Corp, regardless of the format or medium.

**3. Definitions**

*   **Personal Data:** Any data that relates to an identified or identifiable individual. This includes, but is not limited to, names, contact information, identification numbers, financial data, health information, and online identifiers.
*   **Data Principal:** The individual to whom the personal data relates.
*   **Data Fiduciary:** The entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data (in this context, Acme Corp).
*   **Data Processor:** Any entity that processes personal data on behalf of the Data Fiduciary.
*   **Processing:** Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, recording, organization, storage, adaptation, retrieval, use, disclosure, erasure, or destruction.

**4. Principles of Data Processing**

Acme Corp shall adhere to the following principles when processing personal data:

*   **Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency:** Personal data will be processed lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent manner. Data Principals will be informed about the collection and use of their data.
*   **Purpose Limitation:** Personal data will be collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes and not further processed in a manner incompatible with those purposes.
*   **Data Minimization:** Only personal data that is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary for the specified purposes will be collected.
*   **Accuracy:** Personal data will be kept accurate and up-to-date. Inaccurate data will be rectified or erased without delay.
*   **Storage Limitation:** Personal data will be kept in a form that permits identification of Data Principals for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which the personal data is processed.
*   **Integrity and Confidentiality:** Personal data will be processed in a manner that ensures appropriate security, including protection against unauthorized or unlawful processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

**5. Collection of Personal Data**

*   Acme Corp will collect personal data directly from Data Principals or from legitimate third-party sources, with appropriate consent or legal basis.
*   Data Principals will be informed about the types of personal data collected, the purposes of processing, the retention period, and their rights regarding their data.

**6. Processing of Personal Data**

*   Acme Corp will process personal data only for the purposes for which it was collected and for which consent has been obtained or where another lawful basis exists under the DPDPA.
*   Acme Corp will ensure that all processing activities are documented and comply with the DPDPA.

**7. Data Security**

*   Acme Corp will implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
*   These measures include, but are not limited to, access controls, encryption, regular security assessments, and secure data storage practices.
*   Employees are responsible for adhering to Acme Corp's information security policies and procedures.

**8. Data Breach Notification**

*   In the event of a personal data breach, Acme Corp will promptly assess the nature and impact of the breach.
*   Acme Corp will notify the Data Protection Officer (DPO) and, where required by law, the relevant regulatory authorities and affected Data Principals without undue delay.

**9. Data Retention**

*   Personal data will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by applicable laws and regulations.
*   Acme Corp will establish and maintain data retention schedules for different categories of personal data.

**10. Data Principal Rights**

Data Principals have the following rights regarding their personal data:

*   **Right to Access:** The right to access their personal data held by Acme Corp.
*   **Right to Correction:** The right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
*   **Right to Erasure:** The right to request erasure of their personal data under certain circumstances.
*   **Right to Withdraw Consent:** The right to withdraw consent for the processing of their personal data.
*   **Right to Grievance Redressal:** The right to lodge a complaint with Acme Corp's grievance officer regarding the processing of their personal data.

Requests to exercise these rights should be submitted in writing to the Data Protection Officer.

**12. Training and Awareness**

*   All employees who handle personal data will receive regular training on data protection principles, policies, and procedures.
*   Awareness programs will be conducted to ensure employees understand their responsibilities in protecting personal data.

**13. Third-Party Data Processors**

*   Where Acme Corp engages third-party data processors, it will ensure that they provide sufficient guarantees to implement appropriate technical and organizational measures in such a manner that processing will meet the requirements of the DPDPA.
*   Contracts with data processors will include data protection clauses that outline their obligations.

**14. Compliance and Enforcement**

*   Adherence to this policy is mandatory for all individuals covered under its scope.
*   Any violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment, and may also lead to legal action in accordance with applicable laws.

**15. Policy Review**

This Company Data Protection Policy will be reviewed periodically, at least annually, or as required by changes in legislation or business practices, to ensure its continued effectiveness and compliance.
