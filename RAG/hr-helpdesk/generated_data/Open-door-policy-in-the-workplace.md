## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

### **Company Email Usage Policy**

**Policy Brief and Purpose**

The Acme Corp email policy is designed to guide employees on the appropriate use of their corporate email addresses. As a critical communication tool, corporate email is provided for business-related purposes. This policy aims to protect Acme Corp's confidential data, reputation, and technological assets while allowing for reasonable personal use.

**Scope**

This email usage policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who are assigned or granted access to Acme Corp's email system. This includes individually assigned email addresses and departmental accounts.

**Policy Elements**

**1. Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Acme Corp employees are permitted to use their company email for all work-related communications without limitation. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Communicating with current and prospective clients, partners, and vendors.
*   Accessing necessary software and services for business operations.
*   Sharing contact information at professional events for business purposes.
*   Subscribing to industry-related newsletters, online services, or platforms that contribute to professional development.

**2. Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

When using corporate email, employees represent Acme Corp. Therefore, the following actions are strictly prohibited:

*   Sending insulting, discriminatory, or offensive content.
*   Registering for competitive services or products without explicit authorization.
*   Intentionally sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam), especially to colleagues.
*   Signing up for unreliable, illegal, suspect, or disreputable websites or services.
*   Sending emails containing unlawful marketing information or solicitations.
*   Using company email for personal financial gain or any illegal activity.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor corporate email usage to ensure compliance with this policy and to protect company interests.

**3. Personal Use**

Limited personal use of company email is permitted, provided it does not interfere with work responsibilities, violate any company policies, or consume excessive company resources. Examples of acceptable personal use include:

*   Registering for personal development classes or meetups.
*   Sending occasional emails to friends and family, provided no confidential information is disclosed and it does not constitute spam.
*   Downloading safe and appropriate personal content, such as guides or ebooks, provided it does not pose a security risk.

**4. Email Security**

Email is a common vector for malware, data breaches, and phishing attacks. Employees must adhere to the following security measures:

*   **Password Strength:** Set strong passwords with a minimum of eight characters, including a combination of lowercase and uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information (e.g., birthdays) or easily guessable patterns.
*   **Password Management:** Change email passwords every four to five months. Do not write down passwords in insecure locations.
*   **Phishing and Malware Awareness:** Be vigilant about suspicious emails. Employees are instructed to:
    *   Avoid opening attachments or clicking on links from unknown or untrusted senders, especially if the content is vague or unexpected.
    *   Be cautious of clickbait titles or urgent requests.
    *   Verify sender names and email addresses to ensure legitimacy.
    *   Look for stylistic red flags such as grammar mistakes, excessive punctuation, or inconsistent formatting.
*   **Reporting Suspicious Emails:** If an employee is uncertain about the safety of an email, they should contact the Acme Corp IT Security department immediately.
*   **Anti-Malware:** Ensure anti-malware software is kept updated on all company devices.

**5. Email Signature**

Acme Corp encourages employees to use a professional email signature that represents the company well. A standard signature format should be used, including:

*   Employee Name
*   Employee Title
*   Acme Corp (with a link to the company website)
*   Company Phone Number
*   Company Address

Company logos, work-related images, and approved links may be included. Employees can seek assistance from their direct supervisor or the IT department for signature setup.

**6. Disciplinary Consequences**

Employees who fail to adhere to this email usage policy will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment. Examples of violations that may lead to disciplinary action include:

*   Unauthorized transmission of confidential company information.
*   Sending inappropriate, offensive, or harassing emails to coworkers, clients, or partners.
*   Using the corporate email address for any unlawful activity.
