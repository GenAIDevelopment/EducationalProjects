## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Remote Work

**Policy Number:** ACME-REMOTE-001
**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023
**Version:** 1.0

---

### 1. Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a flexible and productive work environment. This Remote Work Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for employees who are approved to work remotely, either on a full-time or hybrid basis. The policy aims to ensure continued operational efficiency, maintain data security, promote employee well-being, and support Acme Corp's commitment to attracting and retaining top talent.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all eligible employees of Acme Corp, regardless of their role or department, who are approved to work remotely. This includes full-time, part-time, and contract employees, subject to their specific employment agreements and the nature of their roles.

### 3. Eligibility for Remote Work

3.1. **Role Suitability:** Eligibility for remote work will be assessed based on the nature of the employee's role and responsibilities. Roles that can be performed effectively outside of the traditional office environment without compromising productivity, collaboration, or security are generally eligible.

3.2. **Performance Standards:** Employees must consistently meet or exceed performance expectations and demonstrate a strong track record of reliability and self-discipline.

3.3. **Managerial Approval:** All remote work arrangements require the approval of the employee's direct manager and may require further approval from department heads or HR, depending on the arrangement.

3.4. **Remote Work Agreement:** Employees approved for remote work must sign a formal Remote Work Agreement, acknowledging their understanding and acceptance of the terms and conditions outlined in this policy.

### 4. Types of Remote Work Arrangements

4.1. **Full-Time Remote:** Employees who work exclusively from a remote location.

4.2. **Hybrid Remote:** Employees who split their work time between a remote location and Acme Corp's office premises, based on an agreed-upon schedule.

### 5. Remote Work Environment and Equipment

5.1. **Workspace:** Employees are responsible for establishing and maintaining a safe, secure, and ergonomic workspace at their remote location. This workspace should be free from distractions and conducive to productive work.

5.2. **Company-Provided Equipment:** Acme Corp will provide necessary equipment (e.g., laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse) to facilitate remote work. This equipment remains the property of Acme Corp and must be used in accordance with the Company Assets Policy.

5.3. **Personal Equipment:** If employees use personal devices for work purposes, they must ensure these devices meet Acme Corp's security standards and are compliant with the BYOD Policy.

5.4. **Internet Connectivity:** Employees are responsible for ensuring reliable and secure internet connectivity at their remote work location. Acme Corp may provide a stipend to offset internet costs, as detailed in the Employee Benefits and Perks policy.

### 6. Data Security and Confidentiality

6.1. **Compliance:** All remote work must comply with Acme Corp's Data Protection Policy, Cybersecurity Policy, and other relevant policies regarding data privacy and security.

6.2. **Secure Networks:** Employees must use secure, password-protected networks for all work-related activities. Public Wi-Fi networks should be avoided for accessing sensitive company data.

6.3. **Confidential Information:** Employees must take all necessary precautions to protect confidential company information, client data, and intellectual property, regardless of their work location.

### 7. Work Hours and Availability

7.1. **Standard Work Hours:** Remote employees are expected to adhere to their agreed-upon work hours, which should align with their team's core working hours to facilitate collaboration.

7.2. **Availability:** Employees must be readily available and responsive during their designated work hours via company-approved communication channels (e.g., email, instant messaging, video conferencing).

7.3. **Time Tracking:** All remote employees must accurately record their working hours using Acme Corp's designated timekeeping system.

### 8. Communication and Collaboration

8.1. **Regular Check-ins:** Managers will conduct regular check-ins with remote employees to discuss work progress, provide feedback, and address any challenges.

8.2. **Team Collaboration:** Employees are expected to actively participate in team meetings, collaborative projects, and other communication activities, utilizing virtual tools effectively.

### 9. Performance Management

9.1. **Performance Expectations:** Performance standards and expectations remain the same for remote employees as for in-office employees. Performance will be evaluated based on results, productivity, and adherence to company policies.

9.2. **Feedback:** Regular performance feedback will be provided to remote employees through formal reviews and informal discussions.

### 10. Expenses and Reimbursement

10.1. **Reimbursable Expenses:** Acme Corp may reimburse employees for pre-approved, work-related expenses incurred while working remotely, as outlined in the Employee Business Expense Policy. This may include internet stipends or costs for necessary office supplies.

10.2. **Non-Reimbursable Expenses:** Personal expenses, home utilities (other than a pre-approved internet stipend), and home maintenance costs are generally not reimbursable.

### 11. Health and Safety

11.1. **Ergonomics:** Employees are responsible for ensuring their remote workspace is ergonomically sound to prevent injuries. Acme Corp may provide resources or guidance on ergonomic best practices.

11.2. **Reporting Incidents:** Any work-related injuries or hazards occurring in the remote workspace must be reported immediately to the employee's manager and the HR department, in accordance with the Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) Policy.

### 12. Policy Violations and Consequences

12.1. **Non-Compliance:** Failure to comply with this Remote Work Policy, or any other applicable Acme Corp policies, may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

12.2. **Revocation of Arrangement:** Acme Corp reserves the right to revoke an employee's remote work arrangement at any time due to business needs, performance issues, or policy violations.

### 13. Policy Review and Amendments

This policy will be reviewed periodically by the HR department and senior management to ensure its continued effectiveness and compliance with relevant laws and regulations. Acme Corp reserves the right to amend or update this policy at any time. Employees will be notified of any significant changes.

### 14. Questions and Support

Employees with questions or requiring clarification regarding this policy should contact the Human Resources department.
