## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

### Company Data Protection Policy

**1. Policy Brief & Purpose**

Acme Corp is committed to treating the personal data of its employees, customers, partners, and other stakeholders with the utmost care, confidentiality, and security. This policy ensures that all personal data collected, processed, stored, and handled by Acme Corp is done so fairly, transparently, and with full respect for individual rights, in accordance with applicable data protection laws in India, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

**2. Scope**

This policy applies to all individuals who provide any amount of personal data to Acme Corp, including but not limited to:

*   Employees of Acme Corp and its subsidiaries.
*   Job candidates.
*   Customers.
*   Suppliers and vendors.
*   Contractors, consultants, and any other external entities acting on behalf of Acme Corp.

**3. Policy Elements**

Acme Corp recognizes that in the course of its operations, it is necessary to obtain and process personal data. This data includes any offline or online information that makes an individual identifiable, such as names, addresses, contact details, identification numbers, financial data, and digital footprints.

Acme Corp collects this information transparently and only with the full cooperation and knowledge of the data subjects. Once this information is available to Acme Corp, the following rules apply:

*   **Accuracy and Up-to-dateness:** Personal data will be accurate and kept up-to-date to the best of our ability.
*   **Fair and Lawful Collection:** Data will be collected fairly and only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes.
*   **Lawful Processing:** Personal data will be processed by Acme Corp within its legal and moral boundaries, and only for the purposes for which it was collected.
*   **Data Security:** Robust technical and organizational measures will be implemented to protect personal data against any unauthorized or illegal access, processing, loss, destruction, or damage by internal or external parties.
*   **Data Minimization:** We will collect only the personal data that is adequate, relevant, and necessary for the specified purposes.
*   **Purpose Limitation:** Personal data will not be processed in a manner that is incompatible with the purposes for which it was originally collected.
*   **Storage Limitation:** Personal data will not be retained for longer than is necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. Acme Corp has established specific retention periods for different types of data.
*   **Data Transfer:** Personal data may be transferred to third parties or international entities only when adequate safeguards are in place to ensure the protection of the data and compliance with applicable data protection laws.
*   **Data Subject Rights:** Individuals have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict the processing of their personal data. Requests to exercise these rights can be made to the designated Data Protection Officer.
*   **Communication of Data Use:** We will inform individuals about which of their data is collected, how it will be processed, and who has access to their information.
*   **Breach Notification:** In the event of a data breach, Acme Corp will promptly assess the impact and, if required, notify the relevant data protection authorities and affected individuals in accordance with applicable laws.

**4. Actions to Exercise Data Protection**

Acme Corp is committed to implementing robust data protection practices, including:

*   Restricting and monitoring access to sensitive data on a need-to-know basis.
*   Developing transparent data collection procedures.
*   Training employees on online privacy, security measures, and data protection principles.
*   Building and maintaining secure networks to protect online data from cyberattacks.
*   Establishing clear procedures for reporting privacy breaches or data misuse.
*   Including appropriate data protection clauses in contracts and communicating data handling statements.
*   Implementing data protection practices such as secure document shredding, secure storage, data encryption, frequent backups, and access authorization controls.
*   Ensuring that data protection provisions are clearly communicated and accessible, for example, on the company website.

**5. Disciplinary Consequences**

All principles described in this policy must be strictly followed. A breach of data protection guidelines will invoke disciplinary and possibly legal action, up to and including termination of employment, depending on the severity and nature of the breach.
