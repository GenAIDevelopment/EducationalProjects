## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Remote Work

**Policy Name:** Remote Work Policy

**Policy Number:** HR-REM-001

**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023

**Revision Date:** N/A

---

### 1. Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a flexible and productive work environment. This Remote Work Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for employees who are approved to work remotely, either on a full-time or hybrid basis. The policy aims to ensure continued operational efficiency, maintain data security, promote employee well-being, and support work-life balance while adhering to all applicable Indian labor laws and regulations.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Acme Corp, regardless of their role or department, who are approved for remote work arrangements. This policy also extends to any contractors or third-party personnel who are granted remote access to Acme Corp systems and data.

### 3. Policy Guidelines

#### 3.1. Eligibility for Remote Work

Eligibility for remote work will be determined based on several factors, including but not limited to:

*   **Job Role Suitability:** The nature of the employee's role and responsibilities must be conducive to remote work without compromising productivity, collaboration, or service delivery. Roles requiring significant physical presence, on-site equipment, or direct client interaction may not be suitable for full-time remote work.
*   **Performance:** Employees must have a demonstrated history of satisfactory performance, reliability, and adherence to company policies.
*   **Infrastructure:** Employees must have a dedicated, safe, and distraction-free workspace at their remote location, equipped with a reliable internet connection capable of supporting Acme Corp's technological requirements.
*   **Compliance:** Employees must agree to comply with all Acme Corp policies, including data security, confidentiality, and code of conduct, while working remotely.

#### 3.2. Types of Remote Work Arrangements

Acme Corp recognizes the following types of remote work arrangements:

*   **Full-Time Remote:** Employees who work exclusively from a location other than an Acme Corp office.
*   **Hybrid Remote:** Employees who split their work time between an Acme Corp office and an approved remote location. The specific schedule (e.g., days in office vs. days remote) will be agreed upon by the employee and their manager.

#### 3.3. Approval Process

1.  **Request:** Employees interested in a remote work arrangement must submit a formal written request to their direct manager, outlining the proposed arrangement (full-time or hybrid) and the rationale.
2.  **Managerial Review:** Managers will assess the request based on the eligibility criteria outlined in Section 3.1 and the team's operational needs.
3.  **HR Review:** If the manager supports the request, it will be forwarded to the Human Resources (HR) department for final review and approval. HR will ensure compliance with legal requirements and company-wide consistency.
4.  **Formal Agreement:** Approved remote work arrangements will be documented in a formal Remote Work Agreement, signed by the employee, manager, and HR. This agreement will detail the terms, expectations, and duration of the arrangement.

#### 3.4. Workspace and Equipment

*   **Employee Responsibility:** Employees are responsible for ensuring their remote workspace is safe, ergonomic, and free from distractions. Acme Corp is not liable for any injuries sustained in the employee's remote workspace.
*   **Company-Provided Equipment:** Acme Corp will provide necessary equipment (e.g., laptop, peripherals) as deemed essential for the role. Employees are responsible for the care and security of company-provided equipment.
*   **Personal Equipment:** If employees use personal equipment for work, they must ensure it meets Acme Corp's security and compatibility standards. Company policies regarding data security and acceptable use apply to all devices used for work purposes.
*   **Internet Connectivity:** Employees are responsible for maintaining a reliable and secure internet connection at their remote location. Acme Corp may provide a stipend or reimbursement for internet services, as detailed in Section 3.8.

#### 3.5. Data Security and Confidentiality

*   **Compliance:** All remote employees must strictly adhere to Acme Corp's Data Protection Policy, Information Security Policy, and Confidentiality Policy.
*   **Secure Networks:** Employees must use secure, password-protected Wi-Fi networks for accessing company systems and data. Public or unsecured networks are prohibited for accessing sensitive company information.
*   **Data Handling:** Confidential and proprietary information must be handled with the same care as if working in the office. Physical documents should be stored securely, and electronic data must be protected through appropriate security measures.
*   **Reporting Breaches:** Any suspected data breaches or security incidents must be reported immediately to the IT Security department.

#### 3.6. Work Hours and Availability

*   **Standard Hours:** Remote employees are expected to maintain their standard work hours as agreed upon in their Remote Work Agreement and role expectations.
*   **Availability:** Employees must be readily available and responsive during their designated work hours, utilizing company-approved communication tools (e.g., email, instant messaging, video conferencing).
*   **Time Tracking:** All employees, including remote workers, are required to accurately record their working hours using Acme Corp's designated timekeeping system. Overtime must be pre-approved by a manager.

#### 3.7. Communication and Collaboration

*   **Regular Check-ins:** Managers will establish regular communication protocols with their remote team members, including team meetings, one-on-one check-ins, and project updates.
*   **Tools:** Employees are expected to utilize company-provided collaboration tools effectively to maintain seamless communication and teamwork.
*   **Visibility:** Managers will ensure that remote employees have equitable opportunities for visibility, participation in meetings, and access to information.

#### 3.8. Compensation and Expenses

*   **Salary:** Compensation will remain unchanged based on the remote work arrangement, unless otherwise stipulated in the Remote Work Agreement or due to a change in role.
*   **Internet/Phone Stipend:** Acme Corp may provide a monthly stipend to eligible remote employees to offset costs associated with internet connectivity and mobile phone usage for business purposes. The amount and eligibility criteria for this stipend will be communicated separately by HR.
*   **Reimbursement:** Expenses incurred for business-related activities while working remotely (e.g., travel for mandatory office visits) will be reimbursed in accordance with Acme Corp's Travel and Expense Policy. Personal expenses related to the remote workspace (e.g., furniture, utilities) are generally not reimbursable unless explicitly stated in the Remote Work Agreement or a separate company policy.

#### 3.9. Performance Management

Performance expectations and evaluation processes remain the same for remote employees as for in-office employees. Managers will focus on results, productivity, and adherence to policies and procedures.

#### 3.10. Return to Office

Acme Corp reserves the right to require employees to return to working from an Acme Corp office location, either temporarily or permanently, based on business needs, changes in role requirements, or policy violations. Employees will be provided with reasonable notice in such circumstances.

### 4. Policy Violations

Failure to comply with this Remote Work Policy, including but not limited to violations of data security, confidentiality, work hour expectations, or performance standards, may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

### 5. Policy Review

This policy will be reviewed annually by the HR department and relevant stakeholders to ensure its continued effectiveness, relevance, and compliance with Indian labor laws and evolving business needs. Amendments will be communicated to all employees.
