## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy

---

### **Company Email Usage Policy**

**Policy Brief and Purpose**

The Company Email Usage Policy at Acme Corp is designed to provide clear guidelines for employees on the appropriate and responsible use of their corporate email addresses. As a critical communication tool for daily operations, this policy aims to safeguard the company's confidential data, protect its reputation, and ensure the security of its technological infrastructure.

**Scope**

This email usage policy applies to all employees, contractors, and any third parties who are assigned or granted access to Acme Corp's email systems. This includes individually assigned email addresses and departmental email accounts.

**Policy Components**

Acme Corp's email system is provided to facilitate business-related communication and to support employees in their professional roles. While we encourage efficient use of this tool, employees are also afforded the freedom to use email for limited personal matters, provided it adheres to the guidelines outlined below.

**Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Employees of Acme Corp are permitted to use their company-assigned email for work-related purposes without undue restriction. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Communicating with current and prospective clients, partners, and vendors.
*   Accessing necessary software and services for job-related functions.
*   Sharing contact information for business purposes at conferences, corporate events, or career fairs.
*   Subscribing to newsletters, online services, or other platforms that contribute to professional development and job performance.

**Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

As corporate email users represent Acme Corp, employees must refrain from the following actions:

*   Sending or forwarding emails containing insulting, discriminatory, or offensive content.
*   Registering for competitive services or platforms without explicit authorization.
*   Intentionally sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam), especially to colleagues.
*   Signing up for unreliable, illegal, suspect, or disreputable websites or services.
*   Sending emails containing unlawful marketing information or solicitations.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor corporate email communications to ensure compliance with this policy and to protect the company's interests.

**Personal Use**

Limited personal use of company email is permitted, provided it does not interfere with work responsibilities or violate any other company policies. Examples of acceptable personal use include:

*   Registering for classes or meetups that may be beneficial for personal growth.
*   Sending non-confidential emails to friends and family, provided they do not constitute spam or violate other policies.
*   Downloading guides, ebooks, or other content for personal use, provided the source is safe and appropriate.

**Email Security**

Email is a common vector for malware, confidentiality breaches, and cyberattacks. Employees must adhere to the following security measures to protect Acme Corp's systems and data:

*   **Strong Passwords:** Set and maintain strong passwords for email accounts. Passwords must have a minimum of eight characters, including a combination of lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information (e.g., birthdays, names) that can be easily guessed.
*   **Password Management:** Do not write down passwords in easily accessible locations. If password storage is necessary, use a secure password manager.
*   **Regular Password Changes:** Change email passwords every four to five months.
*   **Phishing and Malware Awareness:** Be vigilant about emails containing potential malware or employing phishing techniques. Employees are instructed to:
    *   Avoid opening attachments or clicking on links from unknown or suspicious senders, or when the content is inadequately explained.
    *   Be cautious of clickbait titles or urgent requests.
    *   Verify the sender's identity and email address to ensure legitimacy.
    *   Look for stylistic red flags such as grammar mistakes, excessive use of exclamation marks, or inconsistent formatting.

Employees facing any difficulty in identifying whether an email is safe should contact the Acme Corp IT Security department immediately. Employees are also instructed to keep their anti-malware programs updated.

**Email Signature**

Acme Corp encourages employees to create professional email signatures that accurately represent the company. Salespeople and executives who interact with customers or partners should pay particular attention to their email closings. An example of a professional email signature is:

[Employee Name]
[Employee Title], [Company Name with Link]
[Phone Number] | [Company Address]

Company logos, work-related images, and approved links may be included in email signatures. Employees unsure about setting up their signature should consult with their direct supervisor or the IT department.

**Disciplinary Consequences**

Employees who fail to adhere to this email usage policy will be subject to disciplinary action, which may include, but is not limited to, termination of employment. Examples of violations that may lead to disciplinary action include:

*   Using company email to send confidential information without authorization.
*   Sending inappropriate, offensive, or harassing emails to colleagues, clients, or partners.
*   Using the corporate email address for any unlawful activity.
