# Company Data Protection Policy

**1. Policy Statement**

Acme Corp is committed to treating the personal data of its employees, customers, partners, and other stakeholders with the utmost care, confidentiality, and in full compliance with applicable data protection laws in India, including the Personal Data Protection Bill (when enacted). We are dedicated to ensuring that all personal data we collect, process, store, and handle is done so fairly, transparently, and with respect for individual rights.

**2. Purpose**

The purpose of this policy is to:
*   Outline Acme Corp's commitment to data protection and privacy.
*   Establish clear principles and practices for the lawful and ethical handling of personal data.
*   Ensure compliance with all relevant Indian data protection legislation.
*   Protect the rights and freedoms of individuals whose personal data is processed by Acme Corp.
*   Maintain the trust and confidence of our employees, customers, and partners.

**3. Scope**

This policy applies to:
*   All Acme Corp employees, including full-time, part-time, temporary, and contract staff.
*   All contractors, consultants, vendors, and any other third parties acting on behalf of Acme Corp or who may have access to personal data processed by Acme Corp.
*   All personal data collected, processed, stored, or transmitted by Acme Corp, regardless of the format (electronic or physical) or the method of collection.

**4. Definitions**

*   **Personal Data:** Any information that relates to a natural person, which either directly or indirectly, in combination with other information available or likely to be available with Acme Corp, is capable of identifying such person. This includes, but is not limited to, names, addresses, contact details, identification numbers, financial information, employment history, and any other sensitive personal data as defined by law.
*   **Data Controller:** Acme Corp, as the entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data.
*   **Data Processor:** Any third party that processes personal data on behalf of Acme Corp.
*   **Processing:** Any operation or set of operations performed on personal data, whether or not by automated means, such as collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure or destruction.
*   **Sensitive Personal Data:** Personal data that requires a higher level of protection due to its sensitive nature, as defined by applicable laws (e.g., health information, financial details, biometric data, etc.).

**5. Principles of Data Processing**

Acme Corp will adhere to the following principles when processing personal data:

*   **Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency:** Personal data will be processed lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent manner. Individuals will be informed about the collection and use of their data.
*   **Purpose Limitation:** Personal data will be collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes and not further processed in a manner that is incompatible with those purposes.
*   **Data Minimization:** Personal data collected will be adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary in relation to the purposes for which it is processed.
*   **Accuracy:** Personal data will be accurate and, where necessary, kept up to date. Inaccurate personal data will be erased or rectified without delay.
*   **Storage Limitation:** Personal data will be kept in a form that permits identification of data subjects for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which the personal data is processed.
*   **Integrity and Confidentiality:** Personal data will be processed in a manner that ensures appropriate security, including protection against unauthorized or unlawful processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage, using appropriate technical or organizational measures.
*   **Accountability:** Acme Corp will be responsible for, and be able to demonstrate compliance with, the principles outlined in this policy.

**6. Collection of Personal Data**

*   Personal data will only be collected for specific, legitimate business purposes, such as recruitment, employment management, service delivery, customer support, and legal compliance.
*   Individuals will be informed about the types of personal data being collected, the purpose of collection, and the intended recipients of the data at the time of collection.
*   Consent will be obtained where required by law for the processing of personal data, particularly for sensitive personal data.

**7. Processing of Personal Data**

*   Personal data will be processed in accordance with the stated purposes for which it was collected.
*   Acme Corp will ensure that access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.
*   Individuals have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict the processing of their personal data, subject to legal limitations. Requests to exercise these rights should be directed to the designated contact person.

**8. Data Security**

*   Acme Corp will implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.
*   These measures include, but are not limited to, access controls, encryption, regular security assessments, and secure data storage practices.
*   Data Processors engaged by Acme Corp will be contractually obligated to implement equivalent security measures.

**9. Data Breach Notification**

*   In the event of a personal data breach, Acme Corp will promptly assess the nature and impact of the breach.
*   Acme Corp will notify the relevant data protection authorities and affected individuals as required by applicable Data Protection Laws, without undue delay, where feasible.

**10. Data Transfer**

*   Personal data may be transferred to third parties or international entities only when adequate safeguards are in place to ensure the protection of the data and compliance with Data Protection Laws.
*   Cross-border data transfers will adhere to the specific requirements and conditions stipulated by Indian data protection legislation.

**11. Data Retention**

*   Personal data will be retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.
*   Acme Corp will establish and maintain specific data retention periods for different categories of personal data.

**12. Employee Responsibilities**

*   All Acme Corp employees are responsible for understanding and complying with this Data Protection Policy.
*   Employees must handle personal data with care and confidentiality, adhering to security protocols and reporting any suspected data breaches or policy violations immediately.
*   Employees will receive regular training on data protection principles and practices.

**13. Data Protection Officer (DPO)**

Acme Corp will appoint a Data Protection Officer (DPO) responsible for overseeing compliance with this policy and relevant data protection laws. The DPO will act as the primary point of contact for data protection inquiries and concerns.

**14. Review and Updates**

*   This Data Protection Policy will be reviewed periodically (at least annually) and updated as necessary to reflect changes in Acme Corp's data processing activities, business operations, or changes in Data Protection Laws.
*   Employees will be notified of any significant updates to this policy.

**15. Compliance and Consequences**

*   Adherence to this policy is mandatory for all individuals covered under its scope.
*   Any breach of this Data Protection Policy will be taken seriously and may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract, and may also lead to legal action as per applicable laws.
