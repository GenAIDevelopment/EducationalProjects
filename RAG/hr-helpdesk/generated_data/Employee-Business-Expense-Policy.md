## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

### **Company Email Usage Policy**

**Policy Brief and Purpose**

The Acme Corp corporate email system is a vital tool for internal and external communication, essential for our daily operations. This policy is designed to ensure employees understand the appropriate and inappropriate uses of their company email addresses. The primary aims are to safeguard confidential company data from breaches, protect our corporate reputation, and maintain the integrity of our technological infrastructure.

**Scope**

This email usage policy applies to all Acme Corp employees, contractors, and any third parties who are assigned or granted access to company email accounts. This includes individually assigned email addresses and those assigned to departments.

**Policy Components**

Acme Corp provides corporate email accounts to facilitate efficient and professional communication. While the primary purpose is for company-related business, limited personal use is permitted under specific conditions. This policy defines acceptable and unacceptable uses of the corporate email system.

**Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Employees of Acme Corp are permitted to use their company email for all work-related purposes without limitation. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Communicating with current and prospective clients, partners, and vendors.
*   Accessing necessary software and online services required for job functions.
*   Sharing contact information at professional events, conferences, or career fairs for business purposes.
*   Subscribing to industry-relevant newsletters, online services, or platforms that contribute to professional development and job performance.

**Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

As company email users represent Acme Corp, they must refrain from the following actions:

*   Sending insulting, discriminatory, or offensive content.
*   Registering for competitive services or products without explicit authorization.
*   Intentionally sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam), especially to colleagues.
*   Signing up for unreliable, illegal, suspicious, or disreputable websites or services.
*   Sending emails containing unlawful marketing information or solicitations.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor corporate email usage to ensure compliance with this policy and to protect company interests.

**Personal Use**

Limited personal use of company email is permitted, provided it does not interfere with job responsibilities or violate any other company policies. Examples of acceptable personal use include:

*   Registering for personal development classes or meetups that may be beneficial.
*   Sending emails to friends and family, provided no confidential information is disclosed and the emails do not constitute spam.
*   Downloading guides, ebooks, or other content for personal use, provided the sources are safe and appropriate.

**Email Security**

Email is a common vector for malware, confidentiality breaches, and phishing attacks, which can harm Acme Corp's reputation, legal standing, and equipment security. Employees must adhere to the following security measures:

*   **Password Strength:** Set strong passwords with a minimum of eight characters, including a combination of lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information (e.g., birthdays) or easily guessable patterns.
*   **Password Management:** Do not write down passwords in easily accessible locations. Change email passwords every four to five months.
*   **Phishing and Malware Awareness:** Be vigilant about emails containing potential malware or employing phishing techniques. Employees are instructed to:
    *   Avoid opening attachments or clicking on links from unknown or suspicious senders, especially if the content is inadequately explained.
    *   Be wary of clickbait titles or urgent requests.
    *   Verify the sender's identity and email address to ensure legitimacy.
    *   Look for stylistic red flags such as grammar mistakes, excessive use of exclamation marks, or inconsistent capitalization.

If an employee is uncertain about the safety of an email, they should contact the Acme Corp IT Security department for assistance. Employees are also instructed to keep their anti-malware programs updated.

**Email Signature**

Acme Corp encourages employees to use a professional email signature that accurately represents the company. For employees who interact with customers or partners, special attention should be paid to closing emails professionally. A standard email signature should include:

*   Employee Name
*   Employee Title
*   Acme Corp (with a link to the company website)
*   Company Phone Number
*   Company Address

Company logos, work-related images, and approved links may also be included in email signatures. Employees can seek assistance from their direct supervisor or the IT department for setting up their email signatures.

**Disciplinary Consequences**

Employees who fail to adhere to this email usage policy will be subject to disciplinary action, which may include termination of employment. Examples of violations that could lead to disciplinary action include, but are not limited to:

*   Using company email to send confidential information without authorization.
*   Sending inappropriate, offensive, or discriminatory emails to colleagues, clients, or partners.
*   Using the corporate email address for any unlawful activity.
