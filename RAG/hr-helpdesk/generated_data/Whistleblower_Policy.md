## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Remote Work

**Policy Name:** Remote Work Policy

**Policy Number:** HR-REM-001

**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023

**Revision Date:** N/A

---

### 1. Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a flexible and productive work environment that supports employee well-being and operational efficiency. This Remote Work Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for employees who are approved to work remotely, either on a full-time or hybrid basis. The policy aims to ensure that remote work arrangements are managed effectively, maintaining productivity, collaboration, security, and compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Acme Corp who are approved for remote work arrangements. This includes employees working from home, co-working spaces, or other locations outside of Acme Corp's primary office facilities. This policy does not apply to employees whose roles inherently require their physical presence at an Acme Corp facility on a full-time basis, unless specifically approved otherwise.

### 3. Policy Guidelines

#### 3.1. Eligibility for Remote Work

Eligibility for remote work will be determined based on several factors, including but not limited to:

*   **Job Role Suitability:** The nature of the employee's role and responsibilities must be conducive to remote work without compromising productivity, collaboration, or the ability to perform essential job functions. Roles requiring significant on-site presence, direct client interaction, or access to specialized on-site equipment may not be eligible.
*   **Performance:** Employees must have a demonstrated history of strong performance, reliability, and the ability to work independently and manage their time effectively.
*   **Technology and Infrastructure:** Employees must have access to a reliable internet connection and a suitable, secure, and distraction-free workspace at their remote location.
*   **Managerial Approval:** All remote work arrangements require the approval of the employee's direct manager and may also require approval from department heads or HR, depending on the nature of the arrangement.

#### 3.2. Types of Remote Work Arrangements

Acme Corp recognizes the following types of remote work arrangements:

*   **Full-Time Remote:** Employees who work exclusively from a location outside of Acme Corp's offices.
*   **Hybrid Remote:** Employees who split their work time between an Acme Corp office and an approved remote location. The specific schedule (e.g., days in office vs. days remote) will be agreed upon by the employee and their manager.

#### 3.3. Remote Work Environment and Safety

Employees working remotely are responsible for maintaining a safe, secure, and ergonomic workspace. Acme Corp will provide guidance on best practices for setting up a remote workspace. Employees are expected to adhere to all applicable health and safety regulations in their remote work location. Acme Corp is not liable for injuries sustained in an employee's remote workspace, except where mandated by law.

#### 3.4. Technology and Equipment

*   **Company-Provided Equipment:** Acme Corp will provide necessary equipment (e.g., laptops, monitors) for employees approved for remote work. All company-provided equipment remains the property of Acme Corp and must be used in accordance with the Company's IT and Data Security policies.
*   **Personal Equipment:** If employees use personal equipment for work purposes, they must ensure it meets Acme Corp's security and compatibility standards.
*   **Internet Connectivity:** Employees are responsible for ensuring reliable internet connectivity at their remote work location. Acme Corp may provide a stipend to offset internet costs, as outlined in the Compensation section.

#### 3.5. Data Security and Confidentiality

Employees working remotely must strictly adhere to Acme Corp's Data Security and Confidentiality policies. This includes:

*   Protecting company data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.
*   Using secure networks and VPNs as required.
*   Ensuring physical security of company equipment and data.
*   Reporting any suspected security breaches immediately to the IT department.

#### 3.6. Communication and Availability

Remote employees are expected to maintain regular communication with their managers and colleagues. This includes:

*   Being available during agreed-upon core working hours.
*   Responding to communications in a timely manner.
*   Utilizing company-approved communication and collaboration tools.
*   Proactively communicating their availability status (e.g., via status updates on collaboration platforms).

#### 3.7. Performance and Productivity

Performance expectations for remote employees are the same as for in-office employees. Managers will assess performance based on results, productivity, and adherence to job responsibilities. Regular check-ins and performance reviews will be conducted.

#### 3.8. Compensation and Benefits

*   **Salary:** Compensation will remain the same as for comparable in-office roles, unless otherwise specified in a remote work agreement.
*   **Stipend:** Acme Corp may provide a monthly stipend to eligible remote employees to help cover costs associated with remote work, such as internet services or a portion of utility expenses. The amount and eligibility for this stipend will be communicated separately.
*   **Benefits:** All standard employee benefits will continue to apply to remote employees, subject to the terms of the respective benefit plans.

#### 3.9. Travel

Employees approved for remote work may be required to travel to Acme Corp offices or other locations for meetings, training, or other business purposes. Travel expenses will be reimbursed in accordance with Acme Corp's Travel Policy.

#### 3.10. Policy Violations

Failure to comply with this Remote Work Policy, or any other company policies (e.g., IT Security, Code of Conduct), may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

### 4. Application and Approval Process

1.  **Discussion with Manager:** Employees interested in a remote work arrangement should discuss their interest and the suitability of their role with their direct manager.
2.  **Formal Request:** If the manager supports the request, the employee must submit a formal Remote Work Request Form to their manager and HR.
3.  **Review and Approval:** The request will be reviewed by the manager, department head (if applicable), and HR. Approval will be based on the criteria outlined in this policy.
4.  **Remote Work Agreement:** Approved employees will be required to sign a Remote Work Agreement, which will detail the specific terms and conditions of their remote work arrangement, including work location, schedule, equipment, and expectations.
5.  **Regular Review:** Remote work arrangements will be subject to periodic review to ensure continued effectiveness and compliance. Acme Corp reserves the right to modify or revoke remote work arrangements based on business needs or performance issues.

### 5. Policy Review

This Remote Work Policy will be reviewed annually by the Human Resources department and updated as necessary to reflect changes in business needs, technology, and legal requirements.
