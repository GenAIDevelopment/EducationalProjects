## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

---

### **Company Email Usage Policy**

**Policy Brief and Purpose**

The Acme Corp email policy is designed to guide employees on the appropriate and responsible use of their corporate email addresses. Recognizing that company email is a vital tool for daily operations, this policy aims to protect confidential company data, uphold our reputation, and safeguard our technological assets.

**Scope**

This email usage policy applies to all employees, contractors, and any third parties who are assigned or granted access to Acme Corp company email accounts, whether assigned individually or to a department.

**Policy Components**

Acme Corp provides company email as a professional communication tool for business-related purposes. While employees are granted some latitude for limited personal use, the primary function of this resource is for company-related activities. This policy defines acceptable and unacceptable uses of company email.

**Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Employees are permitted to use their company email for all work-related activities. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Communicating with current and prospective clients, partners, and vendors.
*   Accessing necessary software and online services for business operations.
*   Sharing contact information at professional events for business purposes.
*   Subscribing to industry-related newsletters or online services that contribute to professional development.

**Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

As company email users represent Acme Corp, the following actions are strictly prohibited:

*   Sending insulting, discriminatory, or offensive content.
*   Registering for competitive services or products without explicit authorization.
*   Intentionally sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam), especially to colleagues.
*   Signing up for unreliable, illegal, suspect, or disreputable websites or services.
*   Sending emails containing unlawful marketing information or solicitations.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor company email usage.

**Personal Use**

Limited personal use of company email is permitted, provided it does not interfere with job responsibilities or violate any other company policies. Permissible personal use includes:

*   Registering for professional development classes or meetups.
*   Sending occasional, non-confidential emails to friends and family.
*   Downloading safe and appropriate personal content, such as guides or ebooks, provided it does not pose a security risk.

**Email Security**

Company email accounts are a potential vector for malware, data breaches, and cyberattacks. Employees must adhere to the following security measures:

*   **Strong Passwords:** Set and maintain strong passwords with a minimum of eight characters, including a combination of lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information (e.g., birthdays) or easily guessable patterns.
*   **Password Management:** Do not write down passwords. If absolutely necessary, store them securely and change them regularly.
*   **Password Changes:** Change email passwords every four to five months.
*   **Phishing and Malware Awareness:** Be vigilant against emails containing malware or employing phishing techniques.
    *   Do not open attachments or click on links from unknown or suspicious senders, or when the content is inadequately explained.
    *   Be cautious of clickbait titles.
    *   Verify sender identities and email addresses to ensure legitimacy.
    *   Look for stylistic red flags such as grammar mistakes, excessive punctuation, or inconsistent formatting.
*   **Reporting Suspicious Emails:** If an employee is uncertain about the safety of an email, they should contact the IT Security department immediately.
*   **Anti-Malware Updates:** Ensure anti-malware programs are kept up-to-date.

**Email Signature**

Employees are encouraged to use a professional email signature that represents Acme Corp well. Sales and client-facing roles should pay particular attention to their signatures. An example of a professional signature is:

[Employee Name]
[Employee Title]
Acme Corp | [Company Website Link]
[Phone Number] | [Company Address]

Company logos, work-related images, and approved links may be included in email signatures. Assistance with setting up professional signatures can be obtained from the IT department or direct supervisors.

**Disciplinary Consequences**

Employees who fail to comply with this email usage policy will be subject to disciplinary action, which may include termination of employment. Examples of violations leading to disciplinary action include:

*   Unauthorized transmission of confidential company information.
*   Sending inappropriate or offensive emails to colleagues, clients, or partners.
*   Using company email for any unlawful activity.
