## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Remote Work

**Policy Number:** HR-REM-001
**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023
**Version:** 1.0

---

### 1. Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a flexible and productive work environment. This Remote Work Policy outlines the guidelines and expectations for employees who are approved to work remotely, either on a full-time or hybrid basis. The policy aims to ensure continued operational efficiency, maintain data security, and support employee well-being while embracing modern work practices.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Acme Corp who are approved for remote work arrangements. This includes employees working from home, co-working spaces, or other approved remote locations. This policy does not apply to employees whose roles inherently require their physical presence at a company office on a full-time basis, unless a specific hybrid arrangement is approved.

### 3. Policy Guidelines

#### 3.1. Eligibility for Remote Work

Eligibility for remote work will be determined based on several factors, including:

*   **Job Role Suitability:** The nature of the employee's role and responsibilities must be conducive to remote work without negatively impacting productivity, collaboration, or service delivery.
*   **Performance:** Employees must have a demonstrated history of meeting or exceeding performance expectations.
*   **Technology and Infrastructure:** Employees must have access to a reliable internet connection and a suitable, secure, and distraction-free workspace.
*   **Managerial Approval:** All remote work arrangements require the approval of the employee's direct manager and may require further approval from department heads or HR, depending on the arrangement.

#### 3.2. Types of Remote Work Arrangements

Acme Corp recognizes the following types of remote work arrangements:

*   **Full-Time Remote:** Employees who work exclusively from an approved remote location.
*   **Hybrid Remote:** Employees who split their work time between an approved remote location and an Acme Corp office. The specific schedule (e.g., days in office vs. days remote) will be agreed upon by the employee and their manager.

#### 3.3. Workspace and Equipment

*   **Workspace:** Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe, ergonomic, and distraction-free workspace at their remote location. Acme Corp is not liable for injuries sustained in an employee's remote workspace.
*   **Company-Provided Equipment:** Acme Corp will provide necessary equipment (e.g., laptops, monitors) as deemed essential for the employee's role. This equipment remains the property of Acme Corp and must be used in accordance with the Company Assets Policy and Cybersecurity Policy.
*   **Personal Equipment:** If employees use personal equipment for work purposes, they must ensure it meets Acme Corp's security and compatibility standards. Refer to the BYOD Policy for further details.

#### 3.4. Data Security and Confidentiality

*   Employees working remotely must adhere strictly to Acme Corp's Cybersecurity Policy and Data Protection Policy.
*   All company data and confidential information must be protected from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.
*   Employees must use secure networks and company-approved tools for all work-related activities.
*   Remote employees are responsible for ensuring the security of their remote workspace and any company equipment or data within it.

#### 3.5. Work Hours and Availability

*   Remote employees are expected to maintain their standard work hours as agreed upon with their manager, unless a flexible work arrangement is formally approved.
*   Employees must be readily available and responsive during their designated work hours via company-approved communication channels (e.g., email, instant messaging, video conferencing).
*   Managers will establish clear expectations for communication response times and availability.

#### 3.6. Performance Management

*   Performance expectations and evaluation criteria for remote employees are the same as for in-office employees.
*   Managers will conduct regular check-ins and performance reviews to ensure productivity and address any challenges.

#### 3.7. Communication and Collaboration

*   Remote employees are expected to actively participate in team meetings, discussions, and collaborative projects.
*   Acme Corp will provide and support the use of various communication and collaboration tools to facilitate seamless interaction.

#### 3.8. Expenses and Reimbursement

*   Employees may be eligible for reimbursement for pre-approved, work-related expenses incurred while working remotely, in accordance with the Employee Business Expense Policy.
*   A monthly stipend may be provided to eligible remote employees to offset costs associated with remote work (e.g., internet, utilities). Specific details will be communicated separately.

### 4. Application and Approval Process

1.  **Request:** Employees interested in a remote work arrangement must submit a formal request to their direct manager, outlining the proposed arrangement (full-time remote or hybrid) and the rationale.
2.  **Manager Review:** The manager will assess the request based on the eligibility criteria outlined in this policy, considering the employee's role, performance, and team needs.
3.  **Departmental/HR Approval:** If the manager approves the request, it will be forwarded to the relevant department head and/or the HR department for final review and approval.
4.  **Formal Agreement:** Upon approval, a formal Remote Work Agreement will be drafted and signed by the employee, manager, and HR. This agreement will detail the specific terms and conditions of the remote work arrangement.

### 5. Policy Violations

Failure to comply with the terms of this Remote Work Policy, the Remote Work Agreement, or any other related company policies (e.g., Cybersecurity, Company Assets, BYOD) may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment. Acme Corp reserves the right to modify or revoke remote work arrangements based on business needs or policy violations.

### 6. Policy Review

This Remote Work Policy will be reviewed annually by the HR department and updated as necessary to reflect changes in technology, business needs, and legal requirements. Employees will be notified of any significant changes.

### 7. Questions

Employees with questions regarding this policy should contact their direct manager or the HR department.
