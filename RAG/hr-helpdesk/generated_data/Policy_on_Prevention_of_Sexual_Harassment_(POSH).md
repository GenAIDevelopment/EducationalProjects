## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Remote Work

**Policy Title:** Remote Work Policy

**Policy Number:** HR-REM-001

**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023

**Version:** 1.0

---

### 1. Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a flexible and productive work environment. This Remote Work Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for employees who are approved to work remotely, either on a full-time or hybrid basis. The policy aims to ensure continued operational efficiency, maintain data security, and support employee well-being while working outside the traditional office setting.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Acme Corp who are approved to work remotely. This includes employees working from home, co-working spaces, or other approved remote locations. This policy does not apply to employees whose roles inherently require their physical presence at an Acme Corp facility on a full-time basis.

### 3. Policy Guidelines

#### 3.1. Eligibility for Remote Work

*   **Role Suitability:** Eligibility for remote work will be determined based on the nature of the employee's role and responsibilities. Roles that can be performed effectively outside of the office without compromising productivity, collaboration, or security may be considered.
*   **Performance:** Employees must have a demonstrated record of satisfactory performance and adherence to company policies.
*   **Remote Work Environment:** Employees must have a dedicated, safe, and distraction-free workspace at their remote location. They must also have a reliable and secure internet connection capable of supporting their work duties.
*   **Managerial Approval:** All remote work arrangements require the explicit approval of the employee's direct manager and may require further approval from department heads or HR, depending on the arrangement.

#### 3.2. Types of Remote Work Arrangements

Acme Corp recognizes the following types of remote work arrangements:

*   **Full-Time Remote:** Employees who work exclusively from an approved remote location.
*   **Hybrid Remote:** Employees who split their work time between an approved remote location and an Acme Corp office. The specific schedule (e.g., days in office vs. days remote) will be agreed upon by the employee and their manager.

#### 3.3. Expectations for Remote Employees

*   **Productivity and Performance:** Remote employees are expected to maintain the same level of productivity and performance as if they were working in the office. Performance will be evaluated based on established job responsibilities and objectives.
*   **Availability and Communication:** Remote employees must be available and responsive during their agreed-upon work hours. They are expected to maintain regular communication with their manager and team members through approved company channels (e.g., email, instant messaging, video conferencing).
*   **Work Hours:** Remote employees must adhere to their standard work hours, unless an alternative schedule has been formally approved. Overtime must be pre-approved by the manager.
*   **Confidentiality and Data Security:** Remote employees must strictly adhere to Acme Corp's Data Protection and Cybersecurity policies. This includes securing company data, using company-provided or approved devices and networks, and protecting confidential information.
*   **Use of Company Equipment:** Company-issued equipment must be used primarily for business purposes and maintained in good working condition. Employees are responsible for the security of company equipment in their possession.
*   **Reporting:** Remote employees must accurately record their work hours using Acme Corp's designated timekeeping system.

#### 3.4. Managerial Responsibilities

Managers of remote employees are responsible for:

*   Clearly defining performance expectations and work objectives.
*   Ensuring regular communication and feedback with remote team members.
*   Monitoring productivity and addressing any performance concerns promptly.
*   Ensuring remote employees have the necessary resources and support.
*   Promoting an inclusive team environment that values contributions regardless of work location.
*   Ensuring compliance with this Remote Work Policy.

#### 3.5. Remote Work Environment and Safety

*   Employees are responsible for ensuring their remote workspace is safe and ergonomically sound. Acme Corp is not liable for injuries sustained in an employee's remote workspace, except as required by applicable labor laws.
*   Employees should report any work-related injuries or hazards encountered in their remote workspace to HR immediately.

#### 3.6. Expenses and Reimbursement

*   Acme Corp will not be responsible for general home office expenses such as utilities, internet service, or furniture, unless otherwise specified in a formal remote work agreement or mandated by local law.
*   Reimbursement for pre-approved, business-related expenses incurred while working remotely (e.g., specific software, necessary supplies) will be handled in accordance with Acme Corp's Employee Business Expense Policy.

#### 3.7. Termination of Remote Work Arrangement

*   Acme Corp reserves the right to modify or terminate an employee's remote work arrangement at any time due to business needs, performance issues, or policy violations.
*   Employees may also request to end their remote work arrangement by submitting a request to their manager and HR.

### 4. Data Security and Confidentiality

Remote employees must adhere to all Acme Corp policies regarding data security, confidentiality, and acceptable use of company technology. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Using strong, unique passwords for all company accounts and devices.
*   Ensuring company devices are protected with appropriate security software and updates.
*   Avoiding the use of public Wi-Fi networks for accessing sensitive company information.
*   Reporting any suspected security breaches or data loss immediately to the IT department.

### 5. Compliance

All remote employees are expected to comply with this Remote Work Policy, as well as all other applicable Acme Corp policies and procedures, and all relevant Indian labor laws and regulations.

### 6. Policy Review

This Remote Work Policy will be reviewed annually by the Human Resources department and may be updated as necessary to reflect changes in business needs, technology, or legal requirements.

### 7. Questions

Any questions regarding this policy should be directed to the Human Resources department.
