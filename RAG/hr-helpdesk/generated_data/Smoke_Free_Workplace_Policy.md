## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Remote Work

**Policy Name:** Remote Work Policy

**Policy Number:** HR-REM-001

**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023

**Revision Date:** N/A

---

### 1. Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a flexible and productive work environment that supports employee well-being and business continuity. This Remote Work Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for employees who are approved to work remotely, either on a full-time or hybrid basis. The policy aims to ensure that remote work arrangements are managed effectively, maintaining productivity, collaboration, security, and compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Acme Corp who are approved for remote work arrangements. This includes employees working from home, co-working spaces, or other locations outside of Acme Corp's primary office facilities. This policy does not apply to employees whose roles inherently require their physical presence at an Acme Corp facility on a full-time basis, unless a specific hybrid arrangement is approved.

### 3. Policy Guidelines

#### 3.1. Eligibility for Remote Work

Eligibility for remote work will be determined based on several factors, including but not limited to:

*   **Job Role Suitability:** The nature of the employee's role and whether it can be performed effectively outside of the office environment without compromising productivity, collaboration, or security.
*   **Performance:** Employees must have a consistent record of satisfactory performance and demonstrate the ability to work independently and manage their time effectively.
*   **Technology and Infrastructure:** Employees must have access to a reliable internet connection and a suitable, safe, and distraction-free workspace.
*   **Managerial Approval:** All remote work arrangements require the explicit approval of the employee's direct manager and may require further approval from department heads or HR, depending on the arrangement.

#### 3.2. Types of Remote Work Arrangements

Acme Corp recognizes the following types of remote work arrangements:

*   **Full Remote:** Employees who work exclusively from a location outside of Acme Corp's primary office facilities.
*   **Hybrid Remote:** Employees who split their work time between an Acme Corp office and an approved remote location. Specific schedules and days in office will be agreed upon with the manager.

#### 3.3. Remote Work Environment and Safety

Employees working remotely are responsible for establishing and maintaining a safe and ergonomic workspace. Acme Corp will provide guidance on best practices for setting up a remote workspace. Employees are expected to adhere to all applicable health and safety regulations in their remote work location. Acme Corp is not liable for injuries sustained in an employee's remote workspace, unless directly caused by company-provided equipment or negligence on the part of Acme Corp.

#### 3.4. Technology and Equipment

*   **Company-Provided Equipment:** Acme Corp will provide necessary equipment (e.g., laptops, monitors) as deemed appropriate for the role. All company-provided equipment remains the property of Acme Corp and must be used primarily for business purposes.
*   **Personal Equipment:** If employees use personal equipment for work, they must ensure it meets Acme Corp's security standards.
*   **Internet Connectivity:** Employees are responsible for ensuring reliable internet connectivity at their remote work location. Reimbursement for internet services may be provided as per the Employee Expense Policy.

#### 3.5. Security and Confidentiality

Employees working remotely must strictly adhere to Acme Corp's Data Protection, Cybersecurity, and Confidentiality policies. This includes:

*   Protecting company data and confidential information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
*   Using secure networks and company-approved VPNs when accessing company systems.
*   Ensuring physical security of company equipment and data at their remote location.
*   Reporting any security incidents or breaches immediately to the IT department.

#### 3.6. Work Hours and Availability

*   Remote employees are expected to maintain their regular work hours and be available during core business hours, as agreed upon with their manager.
*   Clear communication regarding availability, including breaks and time away from the workspace, is essential. Employees should utilize company-approved communication tools (e.g., calendar, status updates) to indicate their availability.
*   Overtime must be pre-approved by the manager and will be compensated in accordance with Acme Corp's Overtime Policy.

#### 3.7. Performance and Productivity

Performance expectations for remote employees are the same as for in-office employees. Managers will assess performance based on results, productivity, and adherence to job responsibilities. Regular check-ins and performance reviews will be conducted.

#### 3.8. Communication and Collaboration

*   Remote employees are expected to actively participate in team meetings, discussions, and collaborative projects using company-approved communication and collaboration tools.
*   Proactive communication with managers and colleagues is crucial for maintaining team cohesion and project progress.

#### 3.9. Expenses and Reimbursement

Employees may be eligible for reimbursement of certain work-related expenses incurred while working remotely, as outlined in the Employee Business Expense Policy. This may include internet service, office supplies, or other pre-approved items.

#### 3.10. Return to Office

Acme Corp reserves the right to require remote employees to return to the office, either temporarily or permanently, based on business needs, changes in role requirements, or performance concerns. Employees will be provided with reasonable notice in such circumstances.

### 4. Application and Approval Process

1.  **Discussion with Manager:** Employees interested in a remote work arrangement should discuss their interest and suitability with their direct manager.
2.  **Formal Request:** If deemed appropriate, the employee will submit a formal Remote Work Request form to their manager, detailing the proposed arrangement (full remote or hybrid), location, and justification.
3.  **Managerial Review:** The manager will review the request, considering the job role, employee performance, team needs, and operational requirements.
4.  **Departmental/HR Approval:** Approved requests will be forwarded to the relevant department head and/or HR for final review and approval.
5.  **Agreement:** Upon approval, the employee and manager will sign a Remote Work Agreement, outlining the specific terms and conditions of the arrangement.

### 5. Policy Violations

Failure to comply with this Remote Work Policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment. Violations include, but are not limited to:

*   Unauthorized work from a location other than the approved remote work location.
*   Failure to maintain security and confidentiality of company information.
*   Inconsistent performance or productivity.
*   Lack of availability during agreed-upon work hours.
*   Misuse of company equipment or resources.
*   Violation of any other applicable Acme Corp policies.

### 6. Policy Review

This Remote Work Policy will be reviewed annually by the Human Resources department and may be updated as needed to reflect changes in business needs, technology, or legal requirements. Employees will be notified of any significant changes.
