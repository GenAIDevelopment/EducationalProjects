## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Remote Work

**Policy Name:** Remote Work Policy

**Policy Number:** HR-REM-001

**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023

**Version:** 1.0

---

### 1. Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a flexible and productive work environment that supports employee well-being and operational efficiency. This Remote Work Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for employees who are approved to work remotely, either on a full-time or hybrid basis. The policy aims to ensure that remote work arrangements are managed effectively, maintain productivity, uphold data security, and promote a cohesive company culture.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Acme Corp, regardless of their role or department, who are approved for remote work arrangements. This policy does not apply to employees who are required to be on-site for the nature of their job duties.

### 3. Policy Guidelines

**3.1. Eligibility for Remote Work:**

*   **Role Suitability:** Remote work is permitted for roles where the essential job functions can be performed effectively outside of the traditional office environment. This will be determined by department heads in consultation with Human Resources.
*   **Performance Standards:** Employees must consistently meet or exceed performance expectations and demonstrate a strong track record of reliability and self-management.
*   **Infrastructure:** Employees must have a dedicated, safe, and distraction-free workspace with reliable internet connectivity that meets Acme Corp's security and performance standards.
*   **Managerial Approval:** All remote work arrangements require the explicit approval of the employee's direct manager and may be subject to review by Human Resources.

**3.2. Types of Remote Work Arrangements:**

*   **Full-Time Remote:** Employees who work exclusively from a location other than an Acme Corp office.
*   **Hybrid Remote:** Employees who split their work time between an Acme Corp office and an approved remote location. The specific schedule (e.g., days in office vs. days remote) will be agreed upon by the employee and their manager.

**3.3. Remote Work Environment and Responsibilities:**

*   **Workspace:** Employees are responsible for maintaining a safe and ergonomic workspace that is conducive to productivity. Acme Corp is not liable for injuries sustained in an employee's remote workspace.
*   **Internet Connectivity:** Employees must ensure they have a stable and secure internet connection capable of supporting their work responsibilities, including video conferencing and access to company systems.
*   **Data Security and Confidentiality:** Employees must strictly adhere to Acme Corp's Data Protection and Cybersecurity policies. This includes using secure networks, protecting company-issued equipment, and safeguarding confidential information.
*   **Availability and Communication:** Remote employees are expected to be available and responsive during their agreed-upon work hours. Regular communication with managers and team members is crucial. Employees should utilize company-approved communication tools and maintain an up-to-date work status (e.g., on calendar, Slack).
*   **Productivity:** Performance standards and productivity expectations remain the same for remote employees as for on-site employees. Managers will monitor performance through agreed-upon metrics and regular check-ins.

**3.4. Equipment and Resources:**

*   **Company-Issued Equipment:** Acme Corp will provide necessary equipment (e.g., laptops, monitors) as deemed appropriate for the role. Employees are responsible for the care and security of company-issued equipment.
*   **Personal Equipment:** If employees use personal devices for work, they must ensure these devices meet Acme Corp's security standards and are protected by appropriate security measures.
*   **Reimbursement:** Employees may be eligible for reimbursement for certain pre-approved expenses related to remote work, such as internet service or office supplies, as outlined in the Employee Business Expense Policy.

**3.5. Work Hours and Overtime:**

*   **Standard Work Hours:** Remote employees are expected to adhere to their standard work hours as agreed upon with their manager.
*   **Overtime:** Overtime must be pre-approved by the employee's manager and will be compensated in accordance with Acme Corp's Overtime Policy and applicable labor laws.

**3.6. Travel:**

*   Employees working remotely are responsible for their own travel to and from Acme Corp offices for meetings or other business needs, unless otherwise specified in their employment agreement or approved by management. Travel expenses related to required office visits will be handled according to the Company Travel Policy.

### 4. Application and Approval Process

1.  **Discussion with Manager:** Employees interested in a remote work arrangement should discuss their interest and suitability with their direct manager.
2.  **Formal Request:** If deemed appropriate, the employee will submit a formal Remote Work Request form to their manager and Human Resources.
3.  **Review and Approval:** The manager and Human Resources will review the request based on role suitability, employee performance, and the proposed remote work plan. Approval is at the discretion of Acme Corp.
4.  **Agreement:** Approved remote work arrangements will be documented in a Remote Work Agreement, outlining specific terms, expectations, and duration.

### 5. Data Security and Confidentiality

Remote employees must comply with all Acme Corp policies related to data security, confidentiality, and acceptable use of company resources. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Using secure Wi-Fi networks.
*   Locking devices when unattended.
*   Not sharing login credentials.
*   Reporting any suspected security breaches immediately to the IT department.

### 6. Policy Violations

Failure to comply with this Remote Work Policy, including but not limited to, maintaining productivity, adhering to security protocols, or misrepresenting remote work status, may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment. Remote work arrangements may be revoked at any time based on business needs or policy violations.

### 7. Policy Review

This Remote Work Policy will be reviewed annually by the Human Resources department and may be updated as necessary to reflect changes in business needs, technology, or legal requirements. Employees will be notified of any significant changes.
