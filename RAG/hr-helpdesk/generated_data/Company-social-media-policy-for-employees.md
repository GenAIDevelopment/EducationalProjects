# Company Data Protection Policy

**1. Purpose**

This Company Data Protection Policy outlines the principles and practices followed by Acme Corp ("the Company") regarding the collection, processing, storage, and protection of personal data in accordance with the applicable data protection laws in India, including but not limited to the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("Data Protection Laws"). The Company is committed to treating the personal data of its employees, customers, partners, and other stakeholders with the utmost care and confidentiality. This policy ensures that personal data is gathered, stored, and handled fairly, transparently, and with respect for individual rights.

**2. Scope**

This policy applies to all parties who provide any amount of personal data to Acme Corp. This includes, but is not limited to, employees of Acme Corp and its subsidiaries, job candidates, customers, suppliers, partners, and any other external entity that collaborates with Acme Corp or acts on its behalf and may require occasional access to personal data.

**3. Definitions**

*   **Personal Data:** Any information that relates to an identifiable individual, which either directly or indirectly, in combination with other information available or likely to be available with the Company, is capable of identifying such person. This includes, but is not limited to, names, addresses, contact details, identification numbers, financial data, health information, and online identifiers.
*   **Data Controller:** Acme Corp, as the entity that determines the purposes and means of the processing of personal data.
*   **Data Processor:** Any entity that processes personal data on behalf of the Data Controller.
*   **Processing:** Any operation performed on personal data, such as collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure, or destruction.

**4. Collection of Personal Data**

*   **Lawful and Fair Collection:** The Company collects personal data only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes. The data collected is relevant and limited to what is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed.
*   **Transparency:** Personal data is collected in a transparent manner and only with the full cooperation and knowledge of the data subject. Data subjects will be informed about the types of data collected, the purposes of collection, and how their data will be processed.
*   **Consent:** Where required by Data Protection Laws, the Company will obtain explicit consent from data subjects before collecting and processing their personal data.

**5. Processing of Personal Data**

*   **Purpose Limitation:** Personal data is processed only for the purposes for which it was collected and for which consent has been obtained, or as otherwise permitted by Data Protection Laws.
*   **Data Minimization:** The Company ensures that the personal data processed is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary in relation to the purposes for which it is processed.
*   **Accuracy:** The Company takes reasonable steps to ensure that personal data is accurate and kept up-to-date. Data subjects have the right to request correction of inaccurate data.
*   **Individual Rights:** Data subjects have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict the processing of their personal data. Requests to exercise these rights can be made to the designated contact person.

**6. Data Security**

*   **Technical and Organizational Measures:** The Company implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of personal data. This includes measures to prevent unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.
*   **Access Control:** Access to personal data is restricted to employees and authorized third parties on a need-to-know basis, and only for legitimate business purposes.
*   **Regular Audits:** Security measures and data handling practices are subject to regular audits to ensure ongoing effectiveness and compliance.

**7. Data Breach Notification**

*   **Prompt Assessment and Notification:** In the event of a data breach, the Company will promptly assess the impact and, if required by Data Protection Laws, notify the relevant Data Protection Authorities and affected individuals without undue delay.

**8. Data Transfer**

*   **Adequate Safeguards:** Personal data may be transferred to third parties or international entities only when there are adequate safeguards in place to ensure the protection of the data and compliance with the Data Protection Laws.

**9. Data Retention**

*   **Purpose-Specific Retention:** Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. The Company has established specific retention periods for different types of data.

**10. Employee Training and Awareness**

*   **Mandatory Training:** All employees who handle personal data are required to undergo regular training on data privacy principles, security measures, and their responsibilities under this Policy and Data Protection Laws.
*   **Awareness Programs:** The Company will conduct awareness programs to ensure all personnel understand the importance of data protection and their role in safeguarding personal data.

**11. Compliance and Enforcement**

*   **Policy Adherence:** All employees are required to strictly adhere to this Company Data Protection Policy.
*   **Disciplinary Consequences:** A breach of data protection guidelines will invoke disciplinary and possibly legal action, up to and including termination of employment, depending on the severity and nature of the violation.

**12. Updates to the Policy**

*   **Periodic Review:** This Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the Company's data processing activities, technological advancements, or changes in Data Protection Laws. Employees will be notified of any updates to this Policy.
