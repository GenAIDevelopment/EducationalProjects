# Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

## 1. Introduction

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a productive, respectful, and inclusive work environment for all its employees. This Employee Handbook serves as a guide to our company policies, procedures, and expectations. It is designed to ensure clarity, promote fairness, and support the professional growth and well-being of every member of our team.

We encourage all employees to read this handbook thoroughly and familiarize themselves with its contents. Should you have any questions or require further clarification on any policy, please do not hesitate to contact the Human Resources department.

## 2. Company Overview

### 2.1. About Acme Corp

Acme Corp is a leading Information Technology company dedicated to delivering innovative solutions and exceptional service to our clients. With a workforce of approximately 10,000 employees, we operate with a commitment to excellence, integrity, and continuous improvement. Our mission is to leverage technology to drive progress and create value for our stakeholders.

### 2.2. Our Values

Our core values guide our actions and decisions:

*   **Integrity:** We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical rigor.
*   **Innovation:** We embrace creativity and continuously seek new ways to improve our products and services.
*   **Excellence:** We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.
*   **Collaboration:** We believe in the power of teamwork and mutual respect.
*   **Inclusivity:** We foster a diverse and equitable workplace where everyone feels valued and respected.
*   **Customer Focus:** We are dedicated to understanding and meeting the needs of our clients.

## 3. Employment Policies

### 3.1. Equal Employment Opportunity Policy

Acme Corp is an equal opportunity employer committed to providing a work environment free from discrimination and harassment. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, marital status, veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by applicable Indian laws. All employment decisions, including recruitment, hiring, promotion, compensation, training, and termination, are based on merit, qualifications, and business needs.

### 3.2. Anti-Discrimination Policy

Acme Corp strictly prohibits any form of discrimination or harassment. This policy applies to all employees, contractors, visitors, customers, and stakeholders. Discrimination is defined as any negative action or attitude directed toward an individual because of protected characteristics such as age, religion, ethnicity/nationality, disability, gender identity/sexual orientation, etc. We are committed to creating a safe and happy workplace for everyone.

### 3.3. Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) Policy

Acme Corp is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and respectful work environment for all employees. This policy prohibits sexual harassment in any form. It outlines the definition of sexual harassment, reporting procedures, investigation processes, and disciplinary actions. All employees are expected to adhere to this policy, and any complaints will be handled with utmost confidentiality and promptness. An Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) has been established to address all POSH-related matters.

### 3.4. Code of Conduct Policy

All Acme Corp employees are expected to adhere to a high standard of professional conduct. This policy outlines expectations for ethical behavior, integrity, honesty, respect for others, lawfulness, competence, and accountability. It covers aspects such as workplace interactions, protection of company property, professional appearance, and adherence to company policies. Violations of the Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

### 3.5. Employee Classification Policy

Acme Corp classifies employees as either "exempt" or "non-exempt" based on applicable labor laws in India, primarily the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA) principles as adapted locally. This classification determines eligibility for overtime pay. Non-exempt employees are eligible for overtime compensation for hours worked beyond their standard workweek, as per legal requirements. Exempt employees are not eligible for overtime pay.

### 3.6. Employee Recruitment and Selection Policy

Acme Corp is committed to a fair and transparent recruitment and selection process. We aim to attract and hire qualified candidates based on merit, skills, and experience, without discrimination. This policy outlines the steps involved in the recruitment process, including job posting, screening, interviewing, and selection. We encourage internal applications for open positions and strive to build a diverse workforce.

### 3.7. Employee Referral Program Policy

Acme Corp values the contributions of its employees and encourages them to refer qualified candidates for open positions. This policy outlines the process for employee referrals, eligibility for referral bonuses, and the conditions for receiving rewards. We believe that our employees are best positioned to identify individuals who align with our company culture and values.

### 3.8. Employee Rehire Policy

Acme Corp may consider rehiring former employees who left the company in good standing. Eligibility for rehire will be assessed on a case-by-case basis, considering the circumstances of their departure, previous performance, and the availability of suitable positions. Former employees interested in rejoining Acme Corp must follow the standard application process.

### 3.9. Employee Termination Policy

This policy outlines the procedures for both voluntary and involuntary terminations of employment. It covers notice periods, final settlements, return of company property, and exit interviews. Acme Corp is committed to handling all terminations with professionalism, respect, and in compliance with Indian labor laws.

### 3.10. Employee Conflict of Interest Policy

Employees are expected to act in the best interest of Acme Corp and avoid situations where personal interests conflict with their professional responsibilities. This policy defines what constitutes a conflict of interest and requires employees to disclose any potential conflicts to management. Examples include engaging in outside employment that competes with the company, accepting gifts that could influence business decisions, or using company resources for personal gain.

### 3.11. Employee Confidentiality Policy

Employees may have access to confidential and proprietary information belonging to Acme Corp, its clients, or partners. This policy requires employees to maintain the confidentiality of such information during and after their employment. Unauthorized disclosure or use of confidential information is strictly prohibited and may lead to disciplinary action, including termination and legal consequences.

### 3.12. Employee Background Check Policy

Acme Corp may conduct background checks on candidates as part of the hiring process, particularly for roles involving financial responsibility, access to sensitive data, or client interaction. This policy outlines the types of checks conducted, the process, and the criteria for evaluating background check results, ensuring compliance with Indian data protection laws.

### 3.13. Workplace Nepotism Policy

Acme Corp is committed to a fair and equitable workplace. This policy prohibits nepotism, which is the practice of favoritism towards relatives or close personal connections in employment decisions. It outlines prohibited relationships in reporting structures and hiring processes to ensure decisions are based on merit and qualifications.

### 3.14. Workplace Violence Policy

Acme Corp is dedicated to providing a safe and secure work environment for all employees. This policy prohibits any form of workplace violence, threats, or aggressive behavior. It outlines forbidden conduct, reporting procedures, and disciplinary actions for violations. Employees are encouraged to report any concerns or incidents to management or HR immediately.

### 3.15. Workplace Visitor Policy

This policy outlines the procedures and expectations for visitors entering Acme Corp premises. All visitors must register upon arrival, state the purpose of their visit, and may be required to wear an identification badge. Access to certain areas may be restricted, and visitors are expected to adhere to all company safety and security guidelines.
