## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

---

### **Company Email Usage Policy**

**Policy Brief and Purpose**

The Company Email Usage Policy at Acme Corp is designed to guide employees on the appropriate and responsible use of their corporate email addresses. Recognizing that email is a critical tool for daily operations, this policy aims to clarify usage limitations, protect confidential company data, and safeguard our organizational reputation and technological assets.

**Scope**

This email usage policy applies to all employees, contractors, and any third parties who are assigned or granted access to Acme Corp's email system. This includes individually assigned email addresses and those assigned to departments or teams.

**Policy Components**

Acme Corp provides corporate email as a vital communication tool to support employees in their professional responsibilities. While the primary use is for company-related purposes, limited personal use is permitted under specific conditions. This policy defines both proper and improper use of the corporate email system.

**Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Employees of Acme Corp are permitted to use their company email for all work-related activities without restriction. Examples of appropriate use include:

*   Communicating with current or prospective clients, partners, and vendors.
*   Accessing necessary software and services for business operations.
*   Sharing contact information at professional conferences, corporate events, or career fairs for business purposes.
*   Subscribing to industry-relevant newsletters, online services, or platforms that contribute to professional development or job performance.

**Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

As corporate email users represent Acme Corp, they must refrain from the following actions:

*   Sending insulting, discriminatory, or offensive content.
*   Registering for competitive services or products without explicit authorization.
*   Intentionally sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam), especially to colleagues.
*   Signing up for unreliable, illegal, suspicious, or disreputable websites or services.
*   Sending emails containing unlawful marketing information or solicitations.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor corporate email usage.

**Personal Use**

Employees may use their corporate email for limited personal matters, provided it does not interfere with work responsibilities or violate any other company policy. Examples of acceptable personal use include:

*   Registering for professional development classes or meetups that may be beneficial.
*   Sending occasional, non-confidential emails to friends and family, ensuring no spamming or disclosure of sensitive information.
*   Downloading job-related or generally safe and appropriate content, such as guides or ebooks, provided it does not pose a security risk.

**Email Security**

Email is a common vector for malware, data breaches, and cyberattacks, which can significantly impact Acme Corp's reputation, legal standing, and operational security. Employees must adhere to the following security measures:

*   **Strong Passwords:** Set and maintain strong passwords with a minimum of eight characters, including a combination of lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information (e.g., birthdays, names).
*   **Password Management:** Passwords should be memorable but not written down in easily accessible locations.
*   **Regular Updates:** Change email passwords every four to five months.
*   **Phishing and Malware Awareness:** Be vigilant against emails containing malware or employing phishing techniques. Employees are instructed to:
    *   Avoid opening attachments or clicking on links from unknown or suspicious senders, especially if the content is vague or unexpected.
    *   Be wary of clickbait titles or urgent requests.
    *   Verify sender names and email addresses to ensure legitimacy.
    *   Identify stylistic red flags such as grammatical errors, excessive punctuation, or inconsistent formatting.

If an employee is uncertain about the safety of an email, they should contact the IT Security department for assistance. Employees are also instructed to keep their anti-malware programs updated.

**Email Signature**

Acme Corp encourages employees to create professional email signatures that accurately represent the company. For roles that involve external communication (e.g., sales, customer service, management), particular attention should be paid to the signature's content and format. An example of a professional email signature is:

[Employee Name]
[Employee Title]
Acme Corp | [Company Website Link]
[Phone Number] | [Company Address]

Company logos, work-related images, and approved links may be included in email signatures. Employees can seek guidance from their direct supervisor or the IT department on setting up their email signatures.

**Disciplinary Consequences**

Employees who fail to comply with this email usage policy will be subject to disciplinary action, which may include termination of employment. Examples of violations that could lead to disciplinary action include:

*   Unauthorized transmission of confidential company information.
*   Sending inappropriate, offensive, or harassing emails to colleagues, clients, or partners.
*   Using the corporate email address for any unlawful activity.
