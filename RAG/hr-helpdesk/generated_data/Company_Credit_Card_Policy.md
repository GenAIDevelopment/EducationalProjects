## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

### **Company Email Usage Policy**

**Policy Brief and Purpose**

The Acme Corp email policy is designed to guide employees on the appropriate use of their corporate email addresses. As a critical communication tool, corporate email is provided for company-related purposes. This policy aims to protect Acme Corp's confidential data, reputation, and technological assets while also outlining guidelines for acceptable personal use.

**Scope**

This email usage policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who are assigned or granted access to Acme Corp's email system. This includes individually assigned email addresses and departmental accounts.

**Policy Components**

Acme Corp provides corporate email as a valuable tool to facilitate efficient business operations. While primarily intended for company-related communication, limited personal use is permitted under specific conditions. However, employees must always remember that they represent Acme Corp when using their corporate email, and their actions reflect on the organization.

**Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Employees are permitted to use their company email for all work-related activities. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Communicating with current and prospective clients, partners, and vendors.
*   Accessing necessary software and online services for business purposes.
*   Sharing contact information at professional events for business networking.
*   Subscribing to industry-related newsletters or online resources that contribute to professional development.

**Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

Employees must refrain from using their corporate email for the following:

*   Sending or forwarding insulting, discriminatory, or offensive content.
*   Registering for competitive services or products without explicit authorization.
*   Intentionally sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam) to colleagues or external parties.
*   Signing up for unreliable, illegal, suspect, or disreputable websites or services.
*   Sending emails containing unlawful marketing information or solicitations.
*   Using company email for personal gain or any activity that violates company policies or laws.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor corporate email usage to ensure compliance with this policy and to protect company assets and interests.

**Personal Use**

Limited personal use of corporate email is allowed, provided it does not interfere with job duties, company operations, or violate any other company policies. Acceptable personal use includes:

*   Registering for personal development classes or professional meetups.
*   Sending occasional, brief emails to friends and family, provided no confidential information is disclosed and it does not constitute spam.
*   Downloading safe and appropriate personal content, such as guides or ebooks, that do not pose a security risk.

**Email Security**

Email is a common vector for security threats. Employees must adhere to the following security practices:

*   **Strong Passwords:** Set and maintain strong passwords for email accounts. Passwords must be a minimum of eight characters and include a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information (e.g., birthdays) or easily guessable sequences.
*   **Password Management:** Change email passwords every four to five months. Do not write down passwords in easily accessible locations.
*   **Phishing and Malware Awareness:** Be vigilant against emails containing malware or employing phishing techniques.
    *   Do not open attachments or click on links from unknown or suspicious senders, or when the content is inadequately explained.
    *   Be cautious of clickbait titles or urgent requests.
    *   Verify the sender's identity and email address to ensure legitimacy.
    *   Look for stylistic red flags such as grammar mistakes, excessive punctuation, or inconsistent formatting.
*   **Reporting Suspicious Emails:** If an employee is uncertain about the safety of an email, they should contact the Acme Corp IT Security department immediately.
*   **Anti-Malware:** Ensure anti-malware programs on company devices are kept updated.

**Email Signature**

Employees are encouraged to use a professional email signature that represents Acme Corp well. A standard signature format should include:

*   Employee Name
*   Employee Title
*   Acme Corp (with a link to the company website)
*   Company Phone Number
*   Company Address

Company logos, work-related images, and approved links may be included. Employees can seek assistance from their direct supervisor or the IT department for signature setup.

**Disciplinary Consequences**

Employees who fail to adhere to this email usage policy will be subject to disciplinary action, which may include:

*   Verbal warning
*   Written warning
*   Suspension
*   Termination of employment

Examples of violations that may lead to disciplinary action, including termination, include:

*   Unauthorized transmission of confidential company information.
*   Sending inappropriate, offensive, or harassing emails to colleagues, clients, or partners.
*   Using corporate email for any unlawful activity.
