# Company Data Protection Policy

**1. Introduction**

This Company Data Protection Policy outlines the principles and practices followed by Acme Corp ("the Company") regarding the collection, processing, storage, and protection of personal data in accordance with the applicable data protection laws in India, including but not limited to the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("Data Protection Laws"). The Company is committed to treating the personal data of its employees, customers, partners, and other stakeholders with the utmost care and confidentiality.

**2. Definitions**

*   **Personal Data:** Any information that relates to an identifiable natural person, which either directly or indirectly, in combination with other information available or likely to be available with the Company, is capable of identifying such person.
*   **Data Controller:** The entity that determines the purposes and means of the processing of personal data. For the purposes of this policy, Acme Corp is the Data Controller.
*   **Data Processor:** The entity that processes personal data on behalf of the Data Controller.
*   **Processing:** Any operation performed on Personal Data, including collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure, or destruction.

**3. Collection of Personal Data**

3.1. The Company collects Personal Data only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes. The data collected is relevant and limited to what is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed.

3.2. Personal Data may be collected through various channels, including but not limited to:
    *   Online forms and website interactions
    *   Emails and other written communications
    *   In-person interactions and interviews
    *   Employment applications and records
    *   Customer and vendor agreements

3.3. The Company will obtain consent for the collection and processing of Personal Data where required by Data Protection Laws, clearly informing individuals about the purpose of data collection.

**4. Processing of Personal Data**

4.1. The Company processes Personal Data in a fair, transparent, and lawful manner. Personal Data is processed only for the purposes for which it was collected and for which consent has been obtained.

4.2. Individuals have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict the processing of their Personal Data. Requests to exercise these rights can be made to the designated Data Protection Officer.

4.3. The Company will ensure that Personal Data is not processed in a manner that is incompatible with the purposes for which it was collected.

**5. Data Security**

5.1. The Company implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of Personal Data. These measures include, but are not limited to:
    *   Access controls and authorization based on the principle of least privilege.
    *   Encryption of sensitive Personal Data.
    *   Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing.
    *   Secure storage practices for both electronic and physical data.

5.2. Access to Personal Data is restricted to employees and authorized third parties on a need-to-know basis, and only for legitimate business purposes.

**6. Data Breach Notification**

6.1. In the event of a data breach involving Personal Data, the Company will promptly assess the impact and, if required by Data Protection Laws, notify the relevant Data Protection Board and affected individuals in accordance with the stipulated timelines and procedures.

6.2. The Company will maintain a record of all data breaches, including their nature, consequences, and the remedial actions taken.

**7. Data Transfer**

7.1. Personal Data may be transferred to third parties or international entities only when there are adequate safeguards in place to ensure the protection of the data and compliance with the Data Protection Laws.

7.2. The Company will ensure that any third-party processors engaged by the Company adhere to data protection standards equivalent to those outlined in this policy.

**8. Data Retention**

8.1. Personal Data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. The Company has established specific retention periods for different types of data, which are documented in the Company's Data Retention Schedule.

8.2. Upon expiry of the retention period, Personal Data will be securely erased or anonymized in accordance with applicable laws and Company procedures.

**9. Updates to the Policy**

9.1. This Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the Company's data processing activities, technological advancements, or changes in the Data Protection Laws. Employees will be notified of any updates to this Policy through appropriate communication channels.

**10. Compliance and Training**

10.1. All employees are required to comply with this Policy and attend regular training sessions on data privacy matters to ensure awareness and understanding of their responsibilities.

10.2. The Company will conduct periodic audits to ensure compliance with this Policy and applicable Data Protection Laws.
