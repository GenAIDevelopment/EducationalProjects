# Company Data Protection Policy

**1. Policy Statement**

Acme Corp is committed to treating the personal data of its employees, customers, partners, and other stakeholders with the utmost care, confidentiality, and in full compliance with applicable data protection laws in India, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDPA"). This policy outlines our commitment to fair, transparent, and lawful data processing, safeguarding individual rights and ensuring the security of all personal data we collect, process, store, and transmit.

**2. Purpose**

The purpose of this policy is to:

*   Ensure Acme Corp's compliance with the DPDPA and other relevant data protection regulations.
*   Establish clear principles and procedures for the lawful and ethical handling of personal data.
*   Protect the privacy rights of individuals whose personal data is processed by Acme Corp.
*   Maintain the trust and confidence of our employees, customers, and partners.
*   Define responsibilities for data protection within the organization.

**3. Scope**

This policy applies to all Acme Corp employees, contractors, consultants, temporary staff, and any third parties acting on behalf of Acme Corp who collect, process, store, or transmit personal data. It covers all personal data processed by Acme Corp, regardless of the format or medium in which it is held.

**4. Definitions**

*   **Personal Data:** Any information relating to an identified or identifiable Data Principal. This includes, but is not limited to, names, addresses, contact details, identification numbers, financial information, employment details, and any other information that can directly or indirectly identify an individual.
*   **Data Principal:** The individual to whom the personal data relates.
*   **Data Fiduciary:** The entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data. In this policy, Acme Corp is the Data Fiduciary.
*   **Data Processor:** Any person who processes personal data on behalf of the Data Fiduciary.
*   **Processing:** Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure or destruction.
*   **Consent:** Any freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous indication of the Data Principal's wishes by which they signify their agreement to the processing of their personal data.
*   **Data Breach:** Any unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of personal data.

**5. Principles of Data Processing**

Acme Corp will adhere to the following principles when processing personal data:

*   **Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency:** Personal data will be processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently. Data Principals will be informed about the collection and use of their personal data.
*   **Purpose Limitation:** Personal data will be collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes and will not be further processed in a manner incompatible with those purposes.
*   **Data Minimization:** Personal data collected will be adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed.
*   **Accuracy:** Personal data will be accurate and, where necessary, kept up to date. Inaccurate personal data will be erased or rectified without delay.
*   **Storage Limitation:** Personal data will be kept in a form which permits identification of Data Principals for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which the personal data are processed.
*   **Integrity and Confidentiality:** Personal data will be processed in a manner that ensures appropriate security, including protection against unauthorized or unlawful processing and against accidental loss, destruction or damage.
*   **Accountability:** Acme Corp will be responsible for and be able to demonstrate compliance with the principles outlined in this policy.

**6. Collection and Processing of Personal Data**

*   **Consent:** Acme Corp will obtain explicit Consent from Data Principals before collecting and processing their personal data, unless another lawful basis for processing applies. Consent will be specific, informed, and freely given.
*   **Notice:** Data Principals will be provided with clear and accessible notice regarding the types of personal data collected, the purposes of processing, the retention period, and their rights.
*   **Legitimate Interests:** Where processing is based on legitimate interests, Acme Corp will ensure that these interests are balanced against the rights and freedoms of the Data Principal.
*   **Data Accuracy:** Efforts will be made to ensure the accuracy of personal data. Data Principals have the right to request correction of inaccurate data.

**7. Data Security**

*   **Technical and Organizational Measures:** Acme Corp will implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, accidental loss, destruction, or damage. This includes, but is not limited to, access controls, encryption, regular security assessments, and secure data storage practices.
*   **Data Breach Response:** In the event of a Data Breach, Acme Corp will promptly assess the impact and notify the relevant authorities and affected Data Principals in accordance with the DPDPA requirements.

**8. Data Retention and Disposal**

*   Personal data will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.
*   Secure procedures will be followed for the disposal of personal data when it is no longer needed.

**9. Data Subject Rights**

Data Principals have the following rights regarding their personal data:

*   **Right to Access:** The right to access their personal data held by Acme Corp.
*   **Right to Correction and Erasure:** The right to request the correction of inaccurate personal data and the erasure of personal data under certain circumstances.
*   **Right to Grievance Redressal:** The right to lodge a complaint with Acme Corp regarding the processing of their personal data.
*   **Right to Nominate a Representative:** The right to nominate a representative to exercise their rights in the event of death or incapacity.

Acme Corp will establish clear procedures for Data Principals to exercise these rights.

**10. Data Transfers**

Personal data may be transferred to Data Processors or other third parties only when adequate safeguards are in place to ensure the protection of the data and compliance with the DPDPA. International data transfers will be conducted in accordance with applicable legal requirements.

**11. Training and Awareness**

All employees involved in the processing of personal data will receive regular training on data protection principles, Acme Corp's data protection policies, and their responsibilities. Awareness programs will be conducted to promote a culture of data privacy.

**12. Data Protection Officer (DPO)**

Acme Corp will appoint a Data Protection Officer (DPO) responsible for overseeing data protection strategy and implementation to ensure compliance with data protection laws. The DPO will act as the primary point of contact for Data Principals and regulatory authorities regarding data protection matters.

**13. Policy Review and Updates**

This policy will be reviewed periodically, at least annually, and updated as necessary to reflect changes in data protection laws, organizational practices, or business operations. Any updates will be communicated to all relevant parties.

**14. Compliance and Consequences**

*   **Compliance:** All employees and Data Processors are required to strictly adhere to this policy and all applicable data protection laws.
*   **Consequences of Non-Compliance:** Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract, and may also lead to legal penalties for both the individual and Acme Corp.
