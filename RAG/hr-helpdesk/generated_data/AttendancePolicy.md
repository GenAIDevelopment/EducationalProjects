## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

---

### **Company Email Usage Policy**

**Policy Brief and Purpose**

The Company Email Usage Policy at Acme Corp is designed to provide clear guidelines for employees on the appropriate and responsible use of their corporate email addresses. Recognizing that corporate email is a vital tool for daily operations, this policy aims to safeguard confidential company data, protect our reputation, and ensure the security of our technological assets.

**Scope**

This email usage policy applies to all employees, contractors, and any other individuals who are assigned or granted access to Acme Corp's email system. This includes individually assigned email addresses and any departmental or shared email accounts.

**Policy Components**

Acme Corp's corporate email system is provided to facilitate efficient and effective communication for business-related purposes. While employees are granted a degree of freedom for limited personal use, the primary function of this system is for company operations. This policy defines acceptable and unacceptable uses of corporate email.

**Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Employees of Acme Corp are permitted to use their company email for all work-related communications without limitation. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Communicating with current and prospective clients, partners, and vendors.
*   Accessing necessary software and services for job-related functions.
*   Sharing contact information at professional events, conferences, or career fairs for business purposes.
*   Subscribing to industry-relevant newsletters, online services, or platforms that contribute to professional development and job performance.

**Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

As representatives of Acme Corp, employees must refrain from using their corporate email for any purpose that could negatively impact the company. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Sending insulting, discriminatory, or offensive content.
*   Registering for competitive services or products without explicit authorization.
*   Intentionally sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam), especially to colleagues.
*   Signing up for unreliable, illegal, suspect, or disreputable websites or services.
*   Sending emails containing unlawful marketing information or solicitations.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor corporate email usage to ensure compliance with this policy and to protect company interests.

**Personal Use**

Limited personal use of company email is permitted, provided it does not interfere with job responsibilities, company operations, or violate any other company policies. Examples of acceptable personal use include:

*   Registering for professional development classes or meetups that may be beneficial.
*   Sending occasional, brief emails to friends and family, provided no confidential information is disclosed and it does not constitute spam.
*   Downloading guides, ebooks, or other content for personal use, provided these are from safe and reputable sources.

**Email Security**

Email is a common vector for malware, data breaches, and phishing attacks, which can compromise company reputation, security, and operational integrity. Employees must adhere to the following security practices:

*   **Strong Passwords:** Set and maintain strong passwords for email accounts. Passwords must have a minimum of eight characters, including a combination of lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information (e.g., birthdays) or easily guessable patterns.
*   **Password Management:** Do not write down passwords in easily accessible locations. Change email passwords every four to five months.
*   **Phishing and Malware Awareness:** Be vigilant about emails containing potential malware or employing phishing techniques.
    *   Do not open attachments or click on links from unknown or suspicious senders, or when the content is inadequately explained.
    *   Be cautious of clickbait titles or urgent requests for information.
    *   Verify the sender's identity and email address to ensure legitimacy.
    *   Look for stylistic red flags such as grammar mistakes, excessive use of exclamation marks, or inconsistent formatting.
*   **Reporting Suspicious Emails:** If an employee is uncertain about the safety of an email, they should contact the Acme Corp IT Security department immediately.
*   **Anti-Malware Software:** Ensure that anti-malware programs on company devices are kept updated.

**Email Signature**

Acme Corp encourages employees to use a professional email signature that accurately represents the company. For employees who interact with external parties, such as sales or client-facing roles, a standardized signature is required. An example of a professional email signature is:

[Employee Name]
[Employee Title]
Acme Corp | [Company Website Link]
[Company Phone Number] | [Company Address]

Company logos, work-related images, and approved links may be included in email signatures. Employees unsure about creating or updating their signature should consult their direct supervisor or the IT department.

**Disciplinary Consequences**

Employees who fail to adhere to this email usage policy will be subject to disciplinary action, which may include, but is not limited to, verbal warnings, written warnings, suspension, or termination of employment. Examples of violations that may lead to disciplinary action include:

*   Using company email to send confidential information without authorization.
*   Sending inappropriate, offensive, or harassing emails to coworkers, clients, or partners.
*   Using the corporate email address for any unlawful activity.
