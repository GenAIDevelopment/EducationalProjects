## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

### **Company Email Usage Policy**

**Policy Brief and Purpose**

The Acme Corp corporate email system is a vital tool for business communication. This policy outlines the acceptable and unacceptable uses of company email to ensure data security, protect company reputation, and maintain a productive work environment. It aims to safeguard confidential information and technological assets.

**Scope**

This policy applies to all Acme Corp employees, contractors, and any third parties who are assigned or granted access to a company email account. This includes individually assigned email addresses and those assigned to departments.

**Policy Components**

Acme Corp provides corporate email accounts to facilitate efficient business operations. While the primary use is for company-related purposes, limited personal use is permitted under specific conditions.

**Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Employees are permitted to use their company email for work-related activities without significant limitations. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Communicating with current and prospective clients, partners, and vendors.
*   Accessing necessary software and online services for job functions.
*   Sharing contact information at professional events for business purposes.
*   Subscribing to industry-relevant newsletters or online resources that contribute to professional development.

**Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

As company email users represent Acme Corp, the following uses are strictly prohibited:

*   Sending or forwarding emails containing insulting, discriminatory, or offensive content.
*   Registering for competitive services or products without explicit authorization.
*   Intentionally sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam), especially to colleagues.
*   Signing up for unreliable, illegal, suspect, or disreputable websites or services.
*   Sending emails containing unlawful marketing information or solicitations.
*   Using company email for personal gain or any activity that violates company policy or applicable laws.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor corporate email usage to ensure compliance with this policy.

**Personal Use**

Limited personal use of company email is allowed, provided it does not interfere with job responsibilities or violate any other company policies. Permitted personal use includes:

*   Registering for professional development classes or meetups.
*   Sending occasional, brief emails to friends and family, provided no confidential information is disclosed and it does not constitute spam.
*   Downloading safe and appropriate content for personal use, such as guides or ebooks, that do not pose a security risk.

**Email Security**

Email is a common vector for security threats. Employees must adhere to the following security measures:

*   **Strong Passwords:** Set strong passwords for email accounts, meeting minimum requirements of eight characters, including a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information (e.g., birthdays) or easily guessable sequences.
*   **Password Management:** Change email passwords every four to five months. Do not write down passwords in easily accessible locations.
*   **Phishing and Malware Awareness:** Be vigilant against emails containing malware or employing phishing techniques.
    *   Do not open attachments or click on links from unknown or suspicious senders, or when the content is inadequately explained.
    *   Be cautious of clickbait titles or urgent requests for information.
    *   Verify the sender's identity and email address to ensure legitimacy.
    *   Look for stylistic red flags such as grammar mistakes, excessive punctuation, or inconsistent formatting.
*   **Reporting Suspicious Emails:** If an employee is uncertain about the safety of an email, they should contact the IT Security department immediately.
*   **Anti-Malware Software:** Ensure that anti-malware programs are kept updated on all company devices.

**Email Signature**

Employees are encouraged to use a professional email signature that represents Acme Corp well. A standard signature format should include:

*   Employee Name
*   Employee Title
*   Acme Corp (with a link to the company website)
*   Company Phone Number
*   Company Address

Company logos, work-related images, and approved links may be included. Employees can seek assistance from their direct supervisor or the IT department for signature setup.

**Disciplinary Consequences**

Employees who fail to adhere to this email usage policy will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment. Examples of violations include, but are not limited to:

*   Unauthorized transmission of confidential company information.
*   Sending inappropriate, offensive, or harassing emails to colleagues, clients, or partners.
*   Using the corporate email address for any unlawful activity.
*   Repeatedly violating email security protocols.
