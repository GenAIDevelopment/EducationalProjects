## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Remote Work

**Policy Name:** Remote Work Policy

**Policy Number:** HR-REM-001

**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023

**Revision Date:** N/A

---

### 1. Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a flexible and productive work environment. This Remote Work Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for employees who are approved to work remotely, either on a full-time or hybrid basis. The policy aims to ensure continued operational efficiency, maintain data security, promote employee well-being, and support work-life balance while upholding Acme Corp's commitment to its employees and clients.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Acme Corp who are approved to work remotely, in whole or in part. This includes employees working from home, co-working spaces, or other approved remote locations. This policy does not apply to employees whose roles inherently require their physical presence at an Acme Corp office or client site.

### 3. Policy Guidelines

#### 3.1. Eligibility for Remote Work

Eligibility for remote work will be determined based on several factors, including but not limited to:

*   **Job Role Suitability:** The nature of the employee's role and responsibilities must be conducive to remote work without negatively impacting productivity, collaboration, or service delivery. Roles requiring significant physical presence, on-site equipment, or direct client interaction may not be eligible.
*   **Performance:** Employees must have a consistent record of satisfactory performance and demonstrate the ability to work independently and manage their time effectively.
*   **Technology and Infrastructure:** Employees must have access to a reliable internet connection and a suitable, secure, and distraction-free workspace.
*   **Managerial Approval:** All remote work arrangements require the approval of the employee's direct manager and may also require approval from department heads or HR, depending on the arrangement.

#### 3.2. Types of Remote Work Arrangements

Acme Corp recognizes the following types of remote work arrangements:

*   **Full-Time Remote:** Employees who work exclusively from an approved remote location.
*   **Hybrid Remote:** Employees who split their work time between an approved remote location and an Acme Corp office, based on a pre-agreed schedule.

#### 3.3. Application and Approval Process

1.  **Employee Request:** Employees interested in a remote work arrangement must submit a formal written request to their direct manager, outlining the proposed arrangement (full-time or hybrid), the intended remote work location, and how they will maintain productivity and collaboration.
2.  **Managerial Review:** Managers will review the request, considering job suitability, team needs, and employee performance.
3.  **Departmental/HR Approval:** If the manager approves the request, it will be forwarded to the relevant department head and/or the HR department for final review and approval.
4.  **Formal Agreement:** Upon approval, the employee and Acme Corp will sign a Remote Work Agreement, detailing the terms and conditions of the arrangement, including work hours, communication expectations, equipment usage, and security protocols.

#### 3.4. Work Hours and Availability

*   Remote employees are expected to maintain their standard work hours as agreed upon in their Remote Work Agreement, unless otherwise approved.
*   Employees must be readily available and responsive during their designated work hours via company-approved communication channels (e.g., email, instant messaging, video conferencing).
*   Any changes to work schedules or availability must be communicated to the manager in advance.

#### 3.5. Communication and Collaboration

*   Remote employees are expected to actively participate in team meetings, discussions, and collaborative projects.
*   Proactive communication with managers and colleagues is essential to ensure seamless workflow and team cohesion.
*   Acme Corp will provide access to collaboration tools and platforms to facilitate effective remote communication.

#### 3.6. Equipment and Technology

*   Acme Corp will provide necessary company-owned equipment (e.g., laptop, peripherals) to remote employees, as deemed appropriate for their role.
*   Employees are responsible for maintaining a reliable internet connection at their remote work location. Reimbursement for internet expenses may be provided as per the Employee Expense Policy.
*   Employees must adhere to Acme Corp's IT security policies and procedures to protect company data and systems.

#### 3.7. Data Security and Confidentiality

*   Remote employees must ensure the security and confidentiality of all company data and information accessed or stored remotely.
*   This includes using secure networks, strong passwords, and adhering to all data protection guidelines outlined in Acme Corp's Data Protection Policy and Cybersecurity Policy.
*   Company equipment must be used primarily for business purposes and protected from unauthorized access or damage.

#### 3.8. Workspace and Safety

*   Employees are responsible for establishing a safe, ergonomic, and distraction-free workspace at their remote location.
*   Acme Corp is not liable for injuries sustained in an employee's remote workspace, unless directly related to company-provided equipment or negligence on the part of the company.

#### 3.9. Performance Management

*   Performance expectations for remote employees remain the same as for in-office employees.
*   Performance will be evaluated based on results, productivity, and adherence to company policies and remote work guidelines. Regular check-ins and performance reviews will be conducted.

#### 3.10. Expenses and Reimbursement

*   Reimbursement for approved business-related expenses incurred while working remotely will be handled in accordance with Acme Corp's Employee Expense Policy.

#### 3.11. Termination of Remote Work Arrangement

*   Acme Corp reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate an employee's remote work arrangement at any time, with or without notice, based on business needs, performance issues, or policy violations.
*   Employees may also request to end their remote work arrangement by submitting a request to their manager and HR.

### 4. Employee Responsibilities

*   Adhere to all terms outlined in this policy and the signed Remote Work Agreement.
*   Maintain productivity and meet performance expectations.
*   Ensure a secure and functional remote work environment.
*   Communicate proactively and professionally.
*   Comply with all Acme Corp policies, including IT security, data protection, and confidentiality.
*   Report any changes in remote work location or circumstances to their manager and HR.

### 5. Manager Responsibilities

*   Assess the suitability of roles and employees for remote work.
*   Approve or deny remote work requests based on policy guidelines and business needs.
*   Ensure remote employees have the necessary resources and support.
*   Maintain regular communication and provide feedback to remote employees.
*   Monitor performance and ensure adherence to remote work policies.
*   Address any issues or concerns related to remote work arrangements promptly.

### 6. Policy Violations

Failure to comply with this Remote Work Policy or the terms of the Remote Work Agreement may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

### 7. Policy Review

This policy will be reviewed annually by the HR department and updated as necessary to reflect changes in business needs, technology, and legal requirements.
