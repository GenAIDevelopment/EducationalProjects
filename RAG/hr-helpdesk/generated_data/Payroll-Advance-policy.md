## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Remote Work

**Policy Name:** Remote Work Policy

**Policy Number:** HR-REM-001

**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023

**Revision Date:** N/A

---

### 1. Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a flexible and productive work environment that supports employee well-being and business continuity. This Remote Work Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for employees who are approved to work remotely, either on a full-time or hybrid basis. The policy aims to ensure that remote work arrangements are managed effectively, maintaining productivity, collaboration, security, and compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Acme Corp who are approved for remote work arrangements. This includes employees working from home, co-working spaces, or other locations outside of Acme Corp's designated office premises. This policy does not apply to employees whose roles inherently require their physical presence at an Acme Corp facility on a full-time basis, unless specifically approved otherwise through a formal exception process.

### 3. Policy Guidelines

#### 3.1. Eligibility for Remote Work

Eligibility for remote work will be determined based on several factors, including but not limited to:

*   **Job Role Suitability:** The nature of the employee's role and responsibilities must be conducive to remote work without negatively impacting productivity, collaboration, or service delivery. Roles requiring significant physical presence, hands-on equipment operation, or direct in-person client interaction may not be suitable for remote work.
*   **Performance:** Employees must have a consistent record of satisfactory performance and demonstrate the ability to work independently and manage their time effectively.
*   **Technology and Infrastructure:** Employees must have access to a reliable internet connection and a suitable, secure, and distraction-free workspace at their remote location.
*   **Managerial Approval:** All remote work arrangements require the explicit approval of the employee's direct manager and may require further approval from department heads or HR, depending on the nature of the arrangement.

#### 3.2. Types of Remote Work Arrangements

Acme Corp recognizes the following types of remote work arrangements:

*   **Full-Time Remote:** Employees who work exclusively from a location outside of Acme Corp's offices.
*   **Hybrid Remote:** Employees who split their work time between an Acme Corp office and an approved remote location. The specific schedule (e.g., days in office vs. days remote) will be agreed upon by the employee and their manager.

#### 3.3. Remote Work Environment and Safety

Employees working remotely are responsible for maintaining a safe, healthy, and ergonomic workspace. Acme Corp will provide guidance on best practices for setting up a remote workspace. Employees are expected to adhere to all applicable health and safety regulations in their remote work location. Acme Corp is not liable for injuries sustained in an employee's remote workspace, except where such liability is mandated by law.

#### 3.4. Technology and Equipment

*   **Company-Provided Equipment:** Where applicable, Acme Corp will provide necessary equipment (e.g., laptops, monitors) for remote work. Employees are responsible for the care and security of company-issued equipment.
*   **Personal Equipment:** If employees use personal equipment for work purposes, they must ensure it meets Acme Corp's security and compatibility standards.
*   **Internet Connectivity:** Employees are responsible for ensuring reliable internet access at their remote work location. Reimbursement for internet services may be provided in accordance with Acme Corp's Expense Reimbursement Policy.

#### 3.5. Data Security and Confidentiality

Employees working remotely must strictly adhere to Acme Corp's Data Security and Confidentiality Policies. This includes:

*   Using secure networks and VPNs as required.
*   Protecting company data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.
*   Ensuring physical security of company equipment and sensitive documents.
*   Reporting any suspected security breaches immediately to the IT department.

#### 3.6. Work Hours and Availability

*   Remote employees are expected to maintain their regular work hours as agreed upon with their manager, unless a flexible work arrangement has been formally approved.
*   Employees must be available and responsive during their agreed-upon work hours, utilizing company-approved communication tools.
*   Clear communication regarding availability, including breaks and time away from the workspace, is essential.

#### 3.7. Performance Management

Performance expectations and evaluation criteria for remote employees are the same as for in-office employees. Managers will regularly communicate performance expectations and provide feedback. Performance will be assessed based on results, productivity, and adherence to company policies.

#### 3.8. Communication and Collaboration

Effective communication and collaboration are crucial for remote work success. Employees are expected to:

*   Actively participate in virtual meetings and team discussions.
*   Utilize company-provided communication and collaboration tools effectively.
*   Maintain proactive communication with managers and colleagues regarding work progress, challenges, and availability.

#### 3.9. Expenses and Reimbursement

Employees may be eligible for reimbursement of certain pre-approved expenses incurred while working remotely, in accordance with Acme Corp's Employee Business Expense Policy. This may include internet costs, office supplies, or other necessary work-related expenses.

#### 3.10. Termination of Remote Work Arrangement

Acme Corp reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate remote work arrangements at any time, with or without notice, based on business needs, performance issues, or policy violations. Employees may also request to end their remote work arrangement, subject to managerial approval.

### 4. Procedures

#### 4.1. Requesting Remote Work

1.  Employees interested in a remote work arrangement should discuss their interest with their direct manager.
2.  If the manager deems the role potentially suitable, the employee will be provided with the Remote Work Application Form.
3.  The employee must complete the form, detailing the proposed arrangement (full-time remote or hybrid schedule), remote work location, and confirmation of adequate infrastructure.
4.  The completed form, along with any supporting documentation, should be submitted to the manager for review and approval.
5.  Approved requests will be forwarded to the HR department for final review and record-keeping.

#### 4.2. Remote Work Agreement

Upon approval, employees may be required to sign a Remote Work Agreement, which will outline the specific terms and conditions of their remote work arrangement, including expectations for work hours, availability, security, and performance.

#### 4.3. Onboarding and Training

New employees approved for remote work will receive remote onboarding and training. Existing employees transitioning to remote work may receive additional training on remote work best practices, tools, and security protocols.

### 5. Compliance and Consequences

Adherence to this Remote Work Policy is mandatory for all employees approved for remote work. Failure to comply with the terms of this policy, including but not limited to security protocols, work hour expectations, or performance standards, may result in disciplinary action, up to and including the revocation of remote work privileges, reassignment, or termination of employment.

### 6. Policy Review

This Remote Work Policy will be reviewed annually by the HR department and updated as necessary to reflect changes in business needs, technology, and legal requirements. Employees will be notified of any significant changes to the policy.

### 7. Questions

Any questions or concerns regarding this Remote Work Policy should be directed to the Human Resources department.
