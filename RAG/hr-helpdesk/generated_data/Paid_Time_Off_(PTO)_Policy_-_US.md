## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

---

### **Company Email Usage Policy**

**Policy Brief and Purpose**

The Company Email Usage Policy at Acme Corp is designed to guide employees on the appropriate and responsible use of their corporate email addresses. Recognizing the critical role of email in daily operations, this policy aims to safeguard confidential company data, protect our reputation, and ensure the security of our technological assets.

**Scope**

This email usage policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who are assigned or granted access to Acme Corp's email system. This includes individually assigned email addresses and those assigned to departments.

**Policy Components**

Acme Corp provides corporate email as a vital communication tool for business-related activities. While employees are granted a degree of freedom for limited personal use, the primary purpose of this system is for professional communication. This policy defines acceptable and unacceptable uses of corporate email.

**Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Employees of Acme Corp are permitted to use their company email for all work-related purposes without undue limitation. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Communicating with current and prospective clients, partners, and vendors.
*   Accessing necessary software and services for job functions.
*   Sharing contact information for business purposes at conferences, corporate events, or career fairs.
*   Subscribing to industry-related newsletters, online services, or platforms that contribute to professional development and job performance.

**Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

As corporate email users represent Acme Corp, employees must refrain from the following actions:

*   Sending insulting, discriminatory, or offensive content.
*   Registering for competitive services or products without explicit authorization.
*   Intentionally sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam), especially to colleagues.
*   Signing up for unreliable, illegal, suspect, or disreputable websites or services.
*   Sending emails containing unlawful marketing information or solicitations.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor corporate email usage to ensure compliance with this policy and to protect company assets and interests.

**Personal Use**

Limited personal use of corporate email is permitted, provided it does not interfere with job responsibilities, company productivity, or violate any other company policy. Permissible personal use includes:

*   Registering for professional development classes or meetups that may be beneficial.
*   Sending occasional, brief emails to friends and family, provided no confidential information is disclosed and the communication does not constitute spam.
*   Downloading guides, ebooks, or other content for personal use, provided the source is safe and appropriate.

**Email Security**

Email is a common vector for malware, data breaches, and phishing attacks, which can compromise company reputation, security, and equipment. Employees must adhere to the following security practices:

*   **Password Strength:** Set strong passwords with a minimum of eight characters, including a combination of lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information (e.g., birthdays, names).
*   **Password Management:** Change email passwords every four to five months. Do not write down passwords in easily accessible locations.
*   **Phishing and Malware Awareness:** Be vigilant about emails containing potential malware or employing phishing techniques.
    *   Do not open attachments or click on links from unknown or suspicious senders, or when the content is inadequately explained.
    *   Be cautious of clickbait titles or urgent requests.
    *   Verify the sender's identity and email address to ensure legitimacy.
    *   Look for stylistic red flags such as grammar mistakes, excessive use of exclamation marks, or inconsistent formatting.
*   **Reporting Suspicious Emails:** If an employee is uncertain about the safety of a received email, they should contact the IT Security department immediately.
*   **Anti-Malware Updates:** Ensure anti-malware programs are kept updated.

**Email Signature**

Employees are encouraged to use a professional email signature that accurately represents Acme Corp. The standard signature format should include:

[Employee Name]
[Employee Title], [Company Name]
[Company Phone Number] | [Company Website]

Images, company logos, and work-related videos or links may be included in email signatures, subject to IT department approval. Employees unsure about signature formatting should consult their direct supervisor or the Office Manager.

**Disciplinary Consequences**

Employees who fail to adhere to this email usage policy will be subject to disciplinary action, which may include verbal warnings, written warnings, suspension, or termination of employment. Examples of violations leading to disciplinary action include, but are not limited to:

*   Using company email to send confidential information without authorization.
*   Sending inappropriate, offensive, or discriminatory emails to coworkers, clients, or partners.
*   Using the corporate email address for any unlawful activity.
