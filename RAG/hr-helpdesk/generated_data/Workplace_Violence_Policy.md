## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy

---

### **Company Email Usage Policy**

**1. Policy Brief and Purpose**

The Company Email Usage Policy at Acme Corp is designed to provide clear guidelines for employees on the appropriate and responsible use of their corporate email addresses. As a critical communication tool for daily operations, this policy aims to safeguard the company's confidential data, protect its reputation, and ensure the security of its technological infrastructure.

**2. Scope**

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and any third parties who are assigned or granted access to Acme Corp's email systems. This includes individually assigned email addresses and any departmental or shared email accounts.

**3. Policy Components**

Acme Corp provides corporate email accounts to facilitate business-related communication and enhance employee productivity. While the primary use is for company purposes, limited personal use is permitted under specific conditions. This policy defines acceptable and unacceptable uses of the corporate email system.

**4. Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Employees of Acme Corp are permitted to use their company emails for all work-related purposes without undue limitation. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Communicating with current and prospective clients, partners, and vendors.
*   Accessing necessary software and services for job-related functions.
*   Sharing contact information at professional events, conferences, or career fairs for business networking.
*   Subscribing to industry-relevant newsletters, online services, or platforms that contribute to professional development and job performance.

**5. Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

When using their corporate email, employees represent Acme Corp. Therefore, they must refrain from the following actions:

*   Sending emails containing insulting, discriminatory, defamatory, or offensive content.
*   Registering for competitive services or platforms without explicit authorization.
*   Intentionally sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam), especially to colleagues.
*   Signing up for unreliable, illegal, suspicious, or disreputable websites or services.
*   Sending emails containing unlawful marketing information, solicitations, or chain letters.
*   Using the corporate email for any personal business ventures or activities that could create a conflict of interest.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor the use of its corporate email system to ensure compliance with this policy and to protect company assets and reputation.

**6. Personal Use**

Limited personal use of corporate email is permitted, provided it does not interfere with job duties, violate any company policies, or consume excessive company resources. Permissible personal use includes:

*   Registering for personal development classes, workshops, or meetups that may be beneficial.
*   Sending occasional emails to friends and family, provided no confidential information is disclosed and the communication does not constitute spam.
*   Downloading guides, ebooks, or other content for personal use, provided the source is safe and appropriate.

**7. Email Security**

Email is a common vector for malware, data breaches, and phishing attacks, which can compromise company reputation, legal standing, and equipment security. Employees must adhere to the following security measures:

*   **Password Strength:** Set strong passwords with a minimum of eight characters, including a combination of lowercase and uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using easily guessable personal information (e.g., birthdays, names).
*   **Password Management:** Change email passwords every four to five months. Do not write down passwords in easily accessible locations.
*   **Phishing and Malware Awareness:** Be vigilant about emails containing potential malware or employing phishing techniques. Employees are instructed to:
    *   Not open attachments or click on links from unknown or suspicious senders, or when the content is inadequately explained.
    *   Be cautious of clickbait titles or urgent requests for personal information.
    *   Verify the sender's identity and email address to ensure legitimacy.
    *   Look for stylistic red flags such as grammar mistakes, excessive use of exclamation marks, or capitalization.
*   **Reporting Suspicious Emails:** If an employee is uncertain about the safety of a received email, they should contact the IT Security department immediately.
*   **Anti-Malware Software:** Ensure that anti-malware programs are kept updated on all company-issued devices.

**8. Email Signature**

Employees are encouraged to use a professional email signature that accurately represents Acme Corp. The signature should include:

*   Employee Name
*   Employee Title
*   Company Name (with a link to the company website)
*   Company Phone Number
*   Company Address

Images, company logos, and work-related videos or links may be included if they are professional and approved by the Marketing or IT department. Employees unsure about creating an appropriate signature should consult their direct supervisor or the Office Manager.

**9. Disciplinary Consequences**

Employees who fail to adhere to this email usage policy will be subject to disciplinary action, which may include:

*   Verbal or written warnings.
*   Suspension of email privileges.
*   Termination of employment, particularly in cases involving:
    *   Unauthorized transmission of confidential company information.
    *   Sending inappropriate, offensive, or harassing emails to colleagues, clients, or partners.
    *   Using the corporate email address for any unlawful activity.
    *   Repeated violations of security protocols.

Acme Corp reserves the right to take appropriate disciplinary action based on the severity and frequency of the violation.
