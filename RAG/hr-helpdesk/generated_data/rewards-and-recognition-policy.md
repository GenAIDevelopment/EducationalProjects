## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Data Privacy Act India Compliance

**Policy Number:** ACME-HR-DPA-001
**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023
**Version:** 1.0

---

### 1. Purpose and Brief

Acme Corp is committed to upholding the highest standards of data privacy and protecting the personal data of its employees, customers, partners, and any other individuals whose data we process. This Data Privacy Policy outlines Acme Corp's commitment to complying with the applicable data protection laws in India, including the Personal Data Protection Bill (when enacted), hereinafter referred to as "Data Protection Laws." This policy ensures that all personal data is collected, processed, stored, and protected fairly, transparently, and with respect for individual rights.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all individuals who provide any amount of personal data to Acme Corp, including but not limited to:

*   All employees of Acme Corp and its subsidiaries.
*   Contractors, consultants, and any other external entities collaborating with or acting on behalf of Acme Corp.
*   Job candidates.
*   Customers and clients.
*   Suppliers and vendors.
*   Any other party providing personal data to Acme Corp.

### 3. Definitions

*   **Personal Data:** Any information that relates to a natural person, which either directly or indirectly, in combination with other information available or likely to be available with Acme Corp, is capable of identifying such person. This includes, but is not limited to, names, addresses, contact details, identification numbers, financial data, health information, and online identifiers.
*   **Data Controller:** Acme Corp, as the entity that determines the purposes and means of the processing of personal data.
*   **Data Processor:** Any entity that processes personal data on behalf of Acme Corp.
*   **Processing:** Any operation or set of operations performed on personal data, such as collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure or destruction.

### 4. Collection of Personal Data

*   **Lawful Purpose:** Acme Corp collects personal data only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes. The data collected is relevant and limited to what is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed.
*   **Transparency:** Personal data will be collected in a transparent manner and only with the full cooperation and knowledge of the data subject. Data subjects will be informed about the types of data collected, the purpose of collection, and how their data will be used.
*   **Consent:** Where required by Data Protection Laws, Acme Corp will obtain explicit consent from individuals before collecting and processing their personal data.

### 5. Processing of Personal Data

*   **Fairness and Lawfulness:** Acme Corp processes personal data in a fair, transparent, and lawful manner, strictly for the purposes for which it was collected.
*   **Data Subject Rights:** Individuals have the right to:
    *   Access their personal data.
    *   Rectify inaccurate personal data.
    *   Request erasure of their personal data (subject to legal obligations).
    *   Restrict the processing of their personal data.
    *   Object to the processing of their personal data.
*   **Requests:** Requests to exercise these rights can be made in writing to the designated Data Protection Officer.

### 6. Data Security

*   **Technical and Organizational Measures:** Acme Corp implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of personal data, protecting it against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.
*   **Access Control:** Access to personal data is restricted to employees and authorized Data Processors on a strict need-to-know basis. Access is granted based on job roles and responsibilities.

### 7. Data Breach Notification

*   **Prompt Assessment:** In the event of a data breach, Acme Corp will promptly assess the impact and nature of the breach.
*   **Notification:** If required by Data Protection Laws, Acme Corp will notify the relevant data protection authorities and affected individuals without undue delay.

### 8. Data Transfer

*   **Adequate Safeguards:** Personal data may be transferred to third parties or international entities only when adequate safeguards are in place to ensure the protection of the data and compliance with the Data Protection Laws.

### 9. Data Retention

*   **Necessity:** Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.
*   **Retention Periods:** Acme Corp has established specific retention periods for different types of data, and data will be securely disposed of or anonymized once these periods expire.

### 10. Updates to the Policy

*   **Periodic Review:** This Data Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in Acme Corp's data processing activities, business practices, or amendments to Data Protection Laws.
*   **Notification:** Employees will be notified of any significant updates to this Policy through company communication channels.

### 11. Compliance and Training

*   **Employee Obligation:** All employees are required to comply with this Data Privacy Policy and attend regular training sessions on data privacy matters.
*   **Awareness:** Employees are expected to be aware of their responsibilities regarding the handling and protection of personal data.
