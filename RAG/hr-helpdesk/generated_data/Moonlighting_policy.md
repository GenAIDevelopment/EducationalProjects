# Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy

## Policy Title: Data Privacy Act India Policy

**Policy Number:** ACME-PRIV-001
**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023
**Version:** 1.0

---

### 1. Purpose and Brief

This Data Privacy Policy outlines the principles and practices followed by Acme Corp ("Company") regarding the collection, processing, storage, and protection of personal data in accordance with the applicable data protection laws in India, including but not limited to the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDPA"). The Company is committed to safeguarding the privacy of individuals and ensuring that personal data is handled with the utmost care and confidentiality.

### 2. Definitions

*   **2.1 Personal Data:** Any data that relates to an individual who is directly or indirectly identifiable, having regard to any characteristic, mark or attribute of the data, or any other data of which the data is capable of identifying such individual. This includes sensitive personal data as defined under applicable laws.
*   **2.2 Data Fiduciary:** The entity that determines the purposes and means of the processing of personal data. In this policy, Acme Corp acts as the Data Fiduciary.
*   **2.3 Data Principal:** The individual to whom the personal data relates.
*   **2.4 Processing:** Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure or destruction.
*   **2.5 Consent:** Any freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous indication of the Data Principal's wishes by which they signify their agreement to the processing of their personal data.

### 3. Collection of Personal Data

*   **3.1 Lawful Basis:** The Company collects personal data only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes. The data collected is relevant and limited to what is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed.
*   **3.2 Consent:** Where required by law, the Company will obtain explicit consent from the Data Principal before collecting and processing their personal data. Consent will be sought for specific purposes, and Data Principals will be informed about how their data will be used.
*   **3.3 Transparency:** The Company will inform Data Principals about the types of personal data collected, the purposes of processing, the retention period, and their rights under the DPDPA.
*   **3.4 Data Sources:** Personal data may be collected through various channels, including but not limited to online forms, website interactions, emails, employment applications, customer onboarding processes, and in-person interactions.

### 4. Processing of Personal Data

*   **4.1 Fair and Lawful Processing:** The Company processes personal data in a fair, transparent, and lawful manner. Personal data is processed only for the purposes for which it was collected and in accordance with the DPDPA.
*   **4.2 Data Minimization:** The Company will only collect and process personal data that is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary in relation to the purposes for which it is processed.
*   **4.3 Purpose Limitation:** Personal data will be processed only for the specific purposes for which it was collected and will not be further processed in a manner incompatible with those purposes.
*   **4.4 Data Subject Rights:** Data Principals have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict the processing of their personal data. They also have the right to data portability and the right to withdraw consent. Requests to exercise these rights can be made to the designated contact person.

### 5. Data Security

*   **5.1 Technical and Organizational Measures:** The Company implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of personal data. This includes measures to prevent unauthorized access, accidental loss, destruction, or damage to personal data.
*   **5.2 Access Control:** Access to personal data is restricted to employees and authorized third parties on a need-to-know basis. Access is granted based on job roles and responsibilities.
*   **5.3 Data Protection Training:** Employees who handle personal data will receive regular training on data protection principles, security measures, and their responsibilities under this policy and the DPDPA.

### 6. Data Breach Notification

*   **6.1 Breach Response:** In the event of a personal data breach, the Company will promptly assess the impact and, if required by the DPDPA, notify the relevant Data Protection Board and affected Data Principals without undue delay.
*   **6.2 Investigation:** All data breaches will be investigated to determine the cause, scope, and impact, and to implement corrective actions to prevent future occurrences.

### 7. Data Transfer

*   **7.1 International Transfers:** Personal data may be transferred to third parties or international entities only when there are adequate safeguards in place to ensure the protection of the data and compliance with the DPDPA. Transfers will only occur to countries or entities that have been notified by the Government of India as having adequate data protection measures.

### 8. Data Retention

*   **8.1 Retention Periods:** Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. The Company has established specific retention periods for different types of data, which are documented in the Company's data retention schedule.
*   **8.2 Secure Disposal:** Upon expiry of the retention period, personal data will be securely disposed of or anonymized.

### 9. Updates to the Policy

*   **9.1 Policy Review:** This Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the Company's data processing activities, new technologies, or changes in the DPDPA or other applicable data protection laws.
*   **9.2 Notification of Changes:** Employees will be notified of any significant updates to this Policy.

### 10. Compliance and Responsibilities

*   **10.1 Employee Compliance:** All employees are required to comply with this Policy and attend regular training on data privacy matters.
