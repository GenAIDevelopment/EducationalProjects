## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

### **Company Email Usage Policy**

**Policy Brief and Purpose**

The Acme Corp email policy is designed to ensure the appropriate and secure use of company email accounts. As a vital communication tool, corporate email is provided for business-related purposes. This policy aims to protect confidential company data, maintain our professional reputation, and ensure the integrity of our technological infrastructure.

**Scope**

This policy applies to all Acme Corp employees, contractors, and any third parties who are assigned or granted access to company email accounts. This includes individually assigned email addresses and those assigned to departments.

**Policy Components**

Acme Corp email accounts are provided to facilitate efficient business operations. While primarily for company-related communication, limited personal use is permitted under specific conditions. This policy defines acceptable and unacceptable uses of company email.

**Correct Use of Corporate Email**

Employees are permitted to use their company email for work-related purposes without significant limitations. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Communicating with current and prospective clients, partners, and vendors.
*   Accessing necessary software and online services required for job functions.
*   Sharing contact information at professional events for business purposes.
*   Subscribing to industry-related newsletters, online services, or platforms that contribute to professional development and job performance.

**Inappropriate Use of Corporate Email**

As representatives of Acme Corp, employees must refrain from using company email for the following:

*   Sending insulting, discriminatory, defamatory, or offensive content.
*   Registering for competitive services or products without explicit authorization.
*   Intentionally sending unsolicited bulk emails (spam), especially to colleagues.
*   Signing up for unreliable, illegal, suspect, or disreputable websites or services.
*   Sending emails containing unlawful marketing information or solicitations.
*   Disclosing confidential company information without proper authorization.
*   Engaging in any activity that violates Acme Corp's Code of Ethics or other company policies.

Acme Corp reserves the right to monitor corporate email usage to ensure compliance with this policy and to protect company assets and interests.

**Personal Use**

Limited personal use of company email is permitted, provided it does not interfere with job responsibilities, productivity, or violate any other company policy. Permitted personal use includes:

*   Registering for professional development classes or meetups.
*   Sending occasional, brief emails to friends and family, provided no confidential information is disclosed and it does not constitute spam.
*   Downloading safe and appropriate content for personal use, such as guides or ebooks, that do not pose a security risk.

**Email Security**

Email is a common vector for security threats, including malware, phishing attempts, and data breaches. Employees must adhere to the following security practices:

*   **Password Strength:** Set strong, unique passwords for your company email account. Passwords must meet the company's password complexity requirements (e.g., minimum length, combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols). Avoid using personal information or easily guessable patterns.
*   **Password Changes:** Change your email password regularly, as per the company's cybersecurity guidelines (e.g., every 90 days).
*   **Attachment and Link Caution:** Exercise extreme caution when opening attachments or clicking on links in emails, especially from unknown or suspicious senders. If the content is unclear or seems unusual, do not open it.
*   **Sender Verification:** Verify the sender's identity and email address to ensure legitimacy. Be wary of emails that mimic legitimate senders but have slight variations in the address.
*   **Phishing Awareness:** Be alert for common phishing tactics, such as urgent requests for personal information, grammar errors, excessive use of exclamation marks, or unusual formatting.
*   **Reporting Suspicious Emails:** If you receive an email that you suspect is malicious or a phishing attempt, report it immediately to the IT Security department.
*   **Anti-Malware:** Ensure your company-issued devices have up-to-date anti-malware software installed and active.

**Email Signature**

Employees are encouraged to use a professional email signature that represents Acme Corp well. The standard signature format should include:

*   Employee Name
*   Employee Title
*   Acme Corp (with a link to the company website)
*   Company Phone Number
*   Company Address

Company logos, work-related images, and approved links may be included. Consult with your direct supervisor or the HR department for guidance on creating or updating your email signature.

**Disciplinary Consequences**

Employees who fail to adhere to this email usage policy will be subject to disciplinary action, which may include, but is not limited to, verbal warnings, written warnings, suspension, or termination of employment. Examples of violations that may lead to disciplinary action include:

*   Unauthorized transmission of confidential company information.
*   Sending inappropriate, offensive, or harassing emails to colleagues, clients, or partners.
*   Using the corporate email address for any unlawful activity.
*   Repeatedly violating email security protocols.
